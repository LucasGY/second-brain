# Definition
Ajit Jain is Berkshire Hathaway's senior insurance executive, representing the company's underwriting discipline, risk selection, and insurance judgment culture.
> [[ajit-jain|Ajit Jain]] 是 Berkshire Hathaway 的高级保险业务负责人，代表公司的承保纪律、风险选择和保险判断文化。

## Strategic / Technical Significance
- Jain is the key entity for understanding Berkshire's insurance underwriting philosophy: refuse most risks, define downside first, and only act when risk-adjusted economics are compelling.
  > Jain 是理解 Berkshire 保险承保哲学的关键实体：拒绝大多数风险，先定义下行损失，只有在风险调整后收益足够有吸引力时才行动。
