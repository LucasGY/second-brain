# Definition
Meta (formerly Facebook) is a global social media and technology company operating platforms including Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Threads. The company is strategically focused on AI-driven advertising optimization, the Llama open-source ecosystem, and emerging monetization opportunities in short-form video (Reels) and new product monetization (Threads).
Meta（前身为 Facebook）是一家全球社交媒体和科技公司，运营 Facebook、Instagram、WhatsApp 和 Threads 等平台。公司战略重点包括 AI 驱动的广告优化、Llama 开源生态系统，以及短视频（Reels）和新产品变现（Threads）的新机遇。

## Key Facts

- **Founded:** 2004 as Facebook, rebranded to Meta in 2021
- **Headquarters:** Menlo Park, California
- **CEO:** Mark Zuckerberg
- **Key Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, Threads, Oculus
- **AI Products:** Llama (open-source LLM), Advantage+, Meta AI
- **Revenue Mix:** Primarily advertising (Family of Apps)

## 📅 Evolution Timeline

* **[2025 Q4]:** AI广告效率持续提升，Capex扩张维持高位。四大投行（Barclays、Deutsche Bank、JPMorgan、Morgan Stanley）发布研报关注AI变现路径。
* **[2024-2025]:** Llama开源生态快速发展，成为企业级AI的重要选择
* **[2023]:** 推出Threads对标X/Twitter，用户增长强劲但货币化仍在探索
* **[2021]:** 公司更名为Meta，全面转向"元宇宙"战略
* **[2012-2020]:** Instagram/WhatsApp并购，构建社交帝国
