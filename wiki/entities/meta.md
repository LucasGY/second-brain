# Definition
Meta is a social, advertising, AI, and spatial-computing company whose AI infrastructure spending supports engagement, ads monetization, and model development.
> Meta 是一家覆盖社交、广告、AI 和空间计算的公司，其 AI 基础设施支出服务于用户参与度、广告变现和模型研发。

  > **2026-05-05：** 高盛将 **Meta** 列为 Agentic AI 周期中的优选互联网标的，理由是 AI 推动 engagement 和广告变现，尽管资本开支强度较高。来源：[[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|解码 Agentic Economy]]

## Strategic / Technical Significance
* Meta matters because it is a large AI CapEx spender whose return profile depends on whether AI compute improves advertising yield, user engagement, and future AI products.
  > Meta 重要，因为它是大型 AI 资本开支方，其回报取决于 AI 算力能否改善广告收益、用户参与度和未来 AI 产品。
