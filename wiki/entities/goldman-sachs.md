# Definition
Goldman Sachs is a global investment bank and research publisher whose Global Investment Research division issues equity research used in this wiki as a source of market theses, valuation framing, and sector-level investment narratives.
> 高盛是一家全球投资银行和研究机构，其 Global Investment Research 部门发布的股票研究在本 wiki 中用于记录市场论点、估值框架和行业级投资叙事。

## 📅 Evolution Timeline
* **2026-05-05:** Goldman Sachs framed **Agentic AI** as a combined token-demand and margin-inflection thesis, arguing that global token consumption could rise **24x by 2030** while inference unit economics improve. Source: [[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|Decoding the Agentic Economy]]
  > **2026-05-05：** 高盛将 **Agentic AI** 定义为 token 需求与毛利拐点并行的投资命题，认为全球 token 消耗到 2030 年可能提升 **24 倍**，同时推理单位经济性改善。来源：[[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|解码 Agentic Economy]]
