# Definition
[[goldman-sachs|Goldman Sachs]] is a global financial institution spanning investment banking, sales and trading, asset management, and research, and in this wiki it functions as a recurring external analyst source rather than an operating company under study.
[[goldman-sachs|Goldman Sachs]] 是一家覆盖投资银行、销售交易、资产管理与研究业务的全球金融机构；在本知识库中，它更多被当作持续输出观点的外部分析来源，而不是被研究的经营主体。

## Key Facts
- Goldman Sachs research in this wiki is most useful when it frames valuation, market structure, capital-allocation tradeoffs, and cross-sector comparisons rather than company-level operational details.
  在本知识库中，Goldman Sachs 的研究最有价值之处通常在于估值框架、市场结构、资本配置权衡以及跨行业比较，而非单一公司的经营细节。
- Its strategy notes often combine relative performance, earnings revisions, and balance-sheet metrics into a top-down allocation view.
  它的策略报告经常把相对收益、盈利修正和资产负债表指标结合起来，形成自上而下的配置视角。
- Because Goldman Sachs is a sell-side institution, its work is best treated as a structured hypothesis set rather than as neutral truth.
  由于 Goldman Sachs 属于卖方机构，其研究更适合被视为结构化假设集合，而不是中性的“真相”。
