# Google

## Summary
In this wiki, Google is best understood as a broad product platform embedding AI
into many existing surfaces: Gemini features, Google Finance, media generation,
and other distribution-heavy products. The durable signal is product spread and
surface integration.

在这个 wiki 里，Google 最适合被理解成一个把 AI 嵌入大量既有产品面的广平台：
Gemini 功能、Google Finance、媒体生成，以及其他强调分发的产品。更持久的信号
是产品覆盖面和表面集成能力。

## Key Points
- Google-related X posts show breadth more than depth: many launches, many
  surfaces, many user-facing entrypoints.
- Gemini updates matter partly because they demonstrate how Google uses AI to
  refresh familiar products instead of treating AI as a separate app only.
- Google DeepMind performance claims remain relevant, but the repo should track
  how those capabilities get distributed into products.

- 与 Google 相关的 X 帖子体现的是“广度大于深度”：很多发布、很多产品面、很多
  面向用户的入口。
- Gemini 更新之所以重要，一部分原因是它展示了 Google 如何用 AI 改造熟悉产
  品，而不是把 AI 只当成一个独立应用。
- Google DeepMind 的性能声明当然重要，但这个仓库更应该跟踪这些能力最终如何被
  分发进产品。
