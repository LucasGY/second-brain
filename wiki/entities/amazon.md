# Definition
Amazon is a global technology and commerce company whose AWS cloud platform and custom silicon strategy make it a major AI infrastructure provider.
> Amazon 是一家全球科技与电商公司，其 AWS 云平台和定制芯片战略使其成为重要 AI 基础设施提供商。

## 📅 Evolution Timeline
* **2026-05-05:** Goldman Sachs listed **Amazon** as a preferred hyperscaler exposure, citing AWS revenue compounding, AI workloads, backlog visibility, and custom silicon momentum around **Trainium** and **Graviton**. Source: [[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|Decoding the Agentic Economy]]
  > **2026-05-05：** 高盛将 **Amazon** 列为优选 hyperscaler 标的，理由包括 AWS 收入复合增长、AI 工作负载、积压订单可见度，以及 **Trainium** 和 **Graviton** 定制芯片动能。来源：[[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|解码 Agentic Economy]]

## Strategic / Technical Significance
* Amazon matters because AWS is both a seller of AI compute and an internal beneficiary of falling inference costs.
  > Amazon 重要，因为 AWS 既是 AI 算力卖方，也是推理成本下降的内部受益方。
