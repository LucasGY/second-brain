# Definition
[[amazon|Amazon]] is a diversified technology company spanning retail, cloud infrastructure, logistics, devices, advertising, and emerging industrial systems, and it is treated in this wiki as an operator that links infrastructure scale to customer-experience reinvention.
[[amazon|Amazon]] 是一家横跨零售、云基础设施、物流、设备、广告与新兴工业系统的多元化科技公司；在本知识库中，它被视为一家把基础设施规模与客户体验重塑连接起来的运营者。

## Key Facts
- The current wiki evidence presents Amazon as a company that treats AI, robotics, and broadband connectivity as long-horizon platform bets rather than isolated product lines.
  当前知识库证据把 Amazon 描述为一家将 AI、机器人和宽带连接视为长期平台型押注，而非孤立产品线的公司。
- Amazon's operating model emphasizes running multiple parallel implementation paths, then using shared infrastructure and customer feedback to decide what scales. Source: [[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
  Amazon 的运营模式强调同时推进多条实现路径，再利用共享基础设施与客户反馈来判断哪些路径值得规模化。来源：[[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
- The letter positions large capex outlays, especially around AI infrastructure, as acceptable if they widen future free-cash-flow potential rather than optimize near-term optics.
  这封信把大规模资本开支，尤其是 AI 基础设施相关支出，定义为可以接受的，只要它们能扩大未来自由现金流潜力，而不是只优化短期表象。
