# Definition
[[Amazon]] is a multinational technology and commerce company whose strategic importance in this wiki comes from the way it links consumer products, logistics systems, cloud infrastructure, and AI investment into one operating model.
[[Amazon]] 是一家跨国科技与商业公司；在本知识库里，它的重要性来自于它如何把消费产品、物流系统、云基础设施和 AI 投资整合进同一个运营模型。

## Key Facts
- Amazon operates at very large scale across retail, fulfillment, cloud, advertising, devices, and emerging autonomy businesses.
  亚马逊在零售、履约、云计算、广告、设备和新兴自动驾驶业务上都以超大规模运营。
- Its strategy often relies on accepting short-term margin or cash-flow pressure to secure a stronger long-term market position.
  它的战略常常依赖于承受短期利润率或现金流压力，以换取更强的长期市场位置。
- The company uses shared infrastructure and organizational mechanisms to compound advantages across business lines.
  公司通过共享基础设施和组织机制，让多个业务线之间形成复利优势。
