# Definition
Iran is a key geopolitical actor in the Middle East, playing a critical role in global energy markets through its strategic proximity to the Strait of Hormuz. The country has been subject to extensive international sanctions and maintains ongoing tensions with the United States, while also possessing significant oil and natural gas reserves.

伊朗是中东地区重要的地缘政治参与者，通过其与霍尔木兹海峡的战略邻近位置在全球能源市场中发挥关键作用。该国受到广泛的国际制裁，与美国持续紧张，同时拥有重要的石油和天然气储量。

## Key Facts
- **Capital**: Tehran
- **Population**: ~88 million
- **Strategic Asset**: Adjacent to the Strait of Hormuz - approximately 20% of global oil shipments pass through this waterway
- **Sanctions Status**: Subject to extensive US and international sanctions
- **Key Resources**: Major oil and natural gas producer
