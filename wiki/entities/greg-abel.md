# Definition
Greg Abel is Berkshire Hathaway's CEO after Warren Buffett, positioned as the operator responsible for preserving Berkshire's decentralized culture while improving business execution.
> [[greg-abel|Greg Abel]] 是 Warren Buffett 之后的 Berkshire Hathaway CEO，被定位为在保留 Berkshire 分权文化的同时提升业务执行力的运营型领导者。
