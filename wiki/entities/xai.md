# Definition
xAI is Elon Musk's AI company developing Grok, a family of large language models. Grok 4.3 is integrated into OpenClaw as of May 2026.
> xAI是埃隆·马斯克的AI公司，开发Grok系列大语言模型。Grok 4.3已于2026年5月集成到OpenClaw中。
