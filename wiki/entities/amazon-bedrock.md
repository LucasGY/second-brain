# Definition
[[amazon-bedrock|Amazon Bedrock]] is AWS's managed foundation-model and inference service, and it matters in this wiki because it serves as the clearest product example of Amazon's willingness to re-architect a fast-growing AI system in order to preserve speed at scale.
[[amazon-bedrock|Amazon Bedrock]] 是 AWS 的托管式基础模型与推理服务；它在本知识库中之所以重要，是因为它提供了一个最清晰的产品案例，展示 Amazon 如何为了在规模化条件下保持速度，而重构一套快速增长的 AI 系统。

## Key Facts
- The service is described as AWS's primary and very fast-growing inference layer.
  文中把该服务描述为 AWS 主要且增长极快的推理层。
- Bedrock is tied directly to Amazon's custom silicon strategy, with the letter stating that most Bedrock inference runs on Trainium.
  Bedrock 与 Amazon 的自研芯片战略直接绑定；信中指出，Bedrock 的大部分推理运行在 Trainium 上。
- Its “Mantle” re-architecture is presented as a proof point that small, elite teams can restart core infrastructure quickly without waiting for the whole organization to slow down. Source: [[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
  它的 “Mantle” 重构案例，被当作一个证明点：小而精的团队可以迅速重启核心基础设施，而不必等整个组织慢下来。来源：[[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]

## Recent Developments
- 2026-04-15: The shareholder letter states that Bedrock replaced its original inference engine with a new architecture called Mantle, built by a six-engineer team in 76 days, and uses this episode to illustrate Amazon's broader doctrine of returning to the starting line when scale changes the problem. Source: [[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
  2026-04-15：股东信指出，Bedrock 用名为 Mantle 的新架构替换了原始推理引擎，该系统由一个六人工程团队在 76 天内完成；这段经历被用来说明 Amazon 更广泛的方法论：当规模改变问题本质时，要敢于回到起点。来源：[[20260415_manual_amazon_andy_jassy_shareholder_letter|Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
