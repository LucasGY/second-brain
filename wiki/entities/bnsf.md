# Definition
BNSF is Berkshire Hathaway's railroad subsidiary, discussed in the 2026 annual meeting as a major business needing technology-driven efficiency improvement.
> [[bnsf|BNSF]] 是 Berkshire Hathaway 旗下铁路子公司；在 2026 年股东大会中，它被讨论为需要通过技术驱动效率改善的重要业务。
