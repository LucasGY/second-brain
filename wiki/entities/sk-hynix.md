# SK Hynix

_SK 海力士_


## Summary
SK Hynix appears here as the memory-side expression of AI infrastructure
demand. The recurring signal is that new DRAM capacity is being absorbed by HBM
and that memory is no longer a side supplier story but a core bottleneck layer
in the AI stack.

SK 海力士在这里代表的是 AI 基础设施需求在存储侧的表达。反复出现的信号是：新
增 DRAM 产能正在被 HBM 吸收，存储已经不再是边缘供应商故事，而是 AI 栈里的核心
瓶颈层。

## Key Points
- SK Hynix is the clearest recurring memory name in the current equity feed.
- The most important signal is not just strong demand, but that incremental
  DRAM capacity is being redirected into HBM for AI infrastructure.
- Memory now matters to this wiki as a first-order AI bottleneck, not as a
  peripheral semiconductor detail.

- SK 海力士是当前股票信息流里最反复出现的存储名字。
- 最重要的信号不只是需求强，而是新增 DRAM 产能正在被重新导向 AI 基础设施所需
  的 HBM。
- 对这个 wiki 来说，存储现在是一层一线 AI 瓶颈，而不是半导体叙事里的边角细
  节。
