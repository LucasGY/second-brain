# SK Hynix
SK海力士

## Definition
SK Hynix is a South Korean semiconductor company and the world's second-largest memory chipmaker (after Samsung), specializing in DRAM, NAND flash, and HBM (High Bandwidth Memory). It is a critical upstream supplier to NVIDIA, AMD, and other AI chip manufacturers, and has emerged as a key beneficiary of the AI memory supercycle.
SK海力士是一家韩国半导体公司，是全球第二大内存芯片制造商（仅次于三星），专注于DRAM、NAND闪存和HBM（高带宽内存）。它是NVIDIA、AMD和其他AI芯片制造商的关键上游供应商，已成为AI内存超级周期的关键受益者。

## Strategic / Technical Significance
- SK Hynix is the primary HBM supplier to NVIDIA's GPU line (H100, H200, B100 series), making it a direct beneficiary of AI infrastructure capex.
  SK海力士是NVIDIA GPU系列（H100、H200、B100系列）的主要HBM供应商，使其成为AI基础设施资本支出的直接受益者。
- Q1 2026 results confirm the memory supercycle thesis: AI demand is expanding the total memory addressable market beyond traditional consumer electronics, with inference and Agentic AI creating sustained demand pressure.
  Q1 2026业绩确认了内存超级周期论：AI需求正在将内存总可寻址市场扩大到传统消费电子产品之外，推理和Agentic AI创造了持续的需求压力。
- The 72% operating margin in a historically cyclical, commoditized industry signals a structural shift in memory pricing power, driven by supply concentration and AI demand inelasticity.
  在一个历史上周期性、大宗商品化的行业中实现72%的营业利润率，标志着由供应集中和AI需求刚性驱动的内存定价权的结构性转变。
