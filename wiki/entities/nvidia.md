# NVIDIA

## Summary
### English
NVIDIA functions in this wiki as the demand anchor for much of the AI hardware
stack. Even when the raw posts are informal, they repeatedly imply the same
structure: GPU demand pulls on memory, optics, boards, packaging, and foundry
capacity across the broader supply chain.

### 中文
在这个 wiki 里，NVIDIA 是 AI 硬件栈的需求锚点。即便原始帖子表达得很口语化，
它们反复暗示的结构却很一致：GPU 需求会牵引存储、光模块、基板、封装和晶圆代工等
整条供应链。

## Key Points
### English
- NVIDIA matters here less as a standalone company story and more as the force
  that shapes the rest of the infrastructure stack.
- Supply-chain names in the raw feed are often proxies for NVIDIA-led demand.

### 中文
- 在这里，NVIDIA 的意义更多不是一个孤立公司故事，而是塑造整个基础设施栈的那股
  力量。
- 原始流里许多供应链名字，本质上都是 NVIDIA 需求的代理表达。
