# Definition
Accenture is an IT services and consulting company positioned as a beneficiary of enterprise AI integration, workflow redesign, governance, and change management.
> Accenture 是一家 IT 服务和咨询公司，被定位为企业 AI 集成、工作流重构、治理和变革管理的受益者。
