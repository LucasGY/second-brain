# Definition
GEICO is Berkshire Hathaway's auto insurance subsidiary and a central operating turnaround case in the Greg Abel era.
> [[geico|GEICO]] 是 Berkshire Hathaway 旗下汽车保险子公司，也是 Greg Abel 时代的核心经营改善案例。

## 📅 Evolution Timeline
* **[2026-05-02]:** Abel emphasized GEICO's need to price risk correctly, retain customers, and grow policies in force; policy growth improved but still lagged Progressive. Source: [[20260504_manual_berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting-2026|2026 Berkshire Hathaway Annual Shareholders Meeting]]
  > **[2026-05-02]：** Abel 强调 GEICO 需要正确匹配风险定价、留住客户并恢复保单数量增长；虽然保单增长有所改善，但仍落后于 Progressive。来源：[[20260504_manual_berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting-2026|2026 伯克希尔哈撒韦股东大会]]
* **[2026-05-02]:** GEICO's technology transformation was presented as evidence that Berkshire should move from buying disconnected applications to building integrated technology systems. Source: [[20260504_manual_berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting-2026|2026 Berkshire Hathaway Annual Shareholders Meeting]]
  > **[2026-05-02]：** GEICO 的技术转型被作为证据，说明 Berkshire 应从购买割裂的软件应用转向自建整合型技术系统。来源：[[20260504_manual_berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting-2026|2026 伯克希尔哈撒韦股东大会]]

## Strategic / Technical Significance
- GEICO is the clearest operating test of whether Berkshire can use internal technology, data, and AI-enabled tooling to improve a mature business without abandoning underwriting discipline.
  > GEICO 是检验 Berkshire 能否用内部技术、数据和 AI 工具改善成熟业务，同时不放弃承保纪律的最清晰经营案例。
