# SK Hynix

SK Hynix is the world's second-largest memory chipmaker, based in Icheon, South Korea. Leading producer of HBM memory for AI applications.

SK海力士是全球第二大存储器芯片制造商，总部位于韩国利川。是AI应用HBM存储器的主要生产商。
