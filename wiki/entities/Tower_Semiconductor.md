# Tower Semiconductor

Tower Semiconductor is an Israeli pure-play foundry specializing in analog and photonic semiconductors. Recently acquired by Intel.

Tower Semiconductor 是一家以色列纯晶圆代工厂，专注于模拟和光子半导体。最近被英特尔收购。
