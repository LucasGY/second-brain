# Definition
Cloudflare is an edge network and software infrastructure company positioned as a potential beneficiary of distributed AI inference workloads.
> Cloudflare 是一家边缘网络和软件基础设施公司，被定位为分布式 AI 推理工作负载的潜在受益者。

## Strategic / Technical Significance
* Cloudflare matters because agentic applications may require low-latency, distributed inference close to users and enterprise data flows.
  > Cloudflare 重要，因为 Agentic 应用可能需要靠近用户和企业数据流的低延迟、分布式推理。
