# Jack Welch | 杰克·韦尔奇

Jack Welch (1935-2020) was the legendary CEO of General Electric from 1981 to 2001, known for his aggressive management style and making GE one of the most valuable companies.

杰克·韦尔奇（1935-2020）是通用电气1981年至2001年的传奇CEO，以其激进的管理风格闻名，将GE打造成最有价值的公司之一。

## Management Style | 管理风格
- Known for "workout" culture
- Aggressive cost-cutting when necessary
- Focus on talent allocation to opportunities
