# Definition
Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) is the world's largest contract chipmaker, providing advanced semiconductor manufacturing services for AI, HPC, mobile, and automotive sectors. Published comprehensive 2026 technology roadmap defining the industrial structure of the AI compute era.
> 台积电是全球最大的芯片代工厂，为AI、HPC、移动和汽车行业提供先进半导体制造服务。发布2026年技术路线图，定义AI算力时代的工业结构。

## Strategic / Technical Significance
TSMC's roadmap reveals the AI compute infrastructure is transitioning from pure transistor scaling to system-level integration. Key insights: advanced packaging (CoWoS/SoIC) now determines competitive differentiation as much as process nodes. The decision to delay High-NA EUV until after 2029 demonstrates TSMC's strategy of maximizing value from existing EUV equipment.
> 台积电路线图显示AI算力基础设施正从纯晶体管缩放转向系统级集成。关键洞察：先进封装（CoWoS/SoIC）现在与制程节点同等重要。决定延迟High-NA EUV至2029年后体现了台积电最大化现有EUV设备价值的策略。
