# TSMC (Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company)

TSMC is the world's largest contract chipmaker, headquartered in Hsinchu, Taiwan. Key supplier for Apple, NVIDIA, and major AI chip companies.

台积电是全球最大的芯片代工厂，总部位于台湾新竹。是苹果、英伟达和主要AI芯片公司的重要供应商。
