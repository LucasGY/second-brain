2. Run parallel paths

When the winning route is unclear, Amazon prefers multiple live experiments over tidy debate.当最佳路径不清楚时，Amazon 更愿意并行实验，而不是停留在整洁的争论中。

Why FCF fell / 为什么自由现金流下降

Free cash flow fell from $38B to $11B because AI infrastructure requires cash before monetization: land, power, buildings, chips, servers, and networking gear are purchased 6–24 months before revenue arrives.自由现金流从 380 亿美元降至 110 亿美元，因为 AI 基础设施需要先投入后变现：土地、电力、建筑、芯片、服务器和网络设备通常在收入到来前 6–24 个月先行采购。

FCF compression is framed as timing, not thesis failure.信中将自由现金流压缩解释为时间错配，而不是投资逻辑失败。

Custom silicon flywheel / 定制芯片飞轮

Trainium, Graviton, and Nitro are presented as more than infrastructure inputs: they can lower customer cost, improve AWS margins, and potentially become a new Amazon pillar.Trainium、Graviton 和 Nitro 不只是基础设施投入：它们可以降低客户成本、改善 AWS 利润率，并可能成为 Amazon 新支柱。

Claim: Amazon’s chip run rate is >$20B, or ~$50B on a stand-alone chip-company basis.核心说法：Amazon 芯片业务年化收入超过 200 亿美元，若按独立芯片公司口径约为 500 亿美元。

Best one-line takeaway / 最佳一句话 takeaway

Amazon is asking investors to judge AI-era capex through the AWS playbook: painful short-term cash conversion can become attractive long-term free cash flow if capacity is monetized at scale.Amazon 要求投资者用 AWS 剧本理解 AI 时代资本开支：短期现金转换会痛苦，但如果产能能规模化变现，长期自由现金流会很有吸引力。
