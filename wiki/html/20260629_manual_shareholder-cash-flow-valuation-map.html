1. Profit｜利润

Net income is accrual accounting. It can include non-cash items and revenue not yet collected.

净利润是权责发生制口径，可能包含非现金项目和未收现收入。

2. FCF｜自由现金流

FCF is operating cash flow minus CapEx. It is closer to distributable capacity than net income.

FCF 是经营性现金流减 CapEx，比净利润更接近可分配能力。

3. SCF｜股东现金流

SCF is the part of cash actually returned through dividends and net value-accretive buybacks.

SCF 是通过分红和净增厚价值的回购真正返还给股东的现金。

Core viewpoints｜核心观点

High conversion deserves credit｜高兑现应获估值认可

Coca-Cola-style mature businesses can sustain valuation when most FCF is paid through dividends and buybacks.

类似可口可乐的成熟企业，如果大部分 FCF 通过分红与回购返还，估值可获得支撑。

Trapped cash deserves a haircut｜受困现金应打折

Low-yield balance-sheet cash should not automatically be deducted from market cap at face value if it cannot be distributed.

如果低收益账面现金无法分配给股东，就不应机械按面值从市值中扣除。

Gross buybacks can mislead｜总回购额可能误导

Buybacks used mainly for equity incentives are weaker than cancellation-style buybacks that reduce net share count.

主要用于股权激励的回购，弱于能够减少净股本的注销式回购。

Maturity changes the valuation anchor｜成熟期改变估值锚

When growth slows, shareholder cash-flow realization becomes more important than headline profit certainty.

当增长放缓，股东现金流兑现能力会比表观利润确定性更重要。

ICBC bridge example｜工商银行桥梁例子

If dividends are RMB 110.6bn and dividend yield is 4.2%, implied market value is about RMB 110.6bn / 0.042 = RMB 2.63tn. This is a dividend multiple of about 23.8x, not a PE multiple.

如果总股息为 1106 亿元、股息率为 4.2%，隐含市值约为 1106 亿元 / 0.042 = 2.63 万亿元。这是约 23.8 倍的股息倍数，不是 PE 倍数。

Dividend multiple = Market Cap / Dividends = 1 / Dividend Yield = PE / Dividend Payout Ratio
股息倍数 = 市值 / 分红 = 1 / 股息率 = PE / 股利支付率
