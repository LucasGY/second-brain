# PMI (Purchasing Managers' Index) | 采购经理指数

An indicator of business conditions in the manufacturing and services sectors.

制造业和服务业商业状况的指标。

## Interpretation | 解读
- Above 50: Expansion
- Below 50: Contraction

## Current Situation (March 2026) | 当前情况
- US: 5/7 sectors showing higher activity
- But pace of expansion slowing
