# Core Definition
[[ai-infrastructure-spending|AI Infrastructure Spending]] is the coordinated capex cycle through which hyperscalers and adjacent suppliers fund compute, storage, networking, real estate, and energy systems required to deploy AI at scale.
[[ai-infrastructure-spending|AI Infrastructure Spending]] 指的是 hyperscaler 与相关供应链共同推动的一轮资本开支周期，用于建设大规模部署 AI 所需的算力、存储、网络、地产与能源系统。

## 🛠️ Mechanisms & Architecture
- **Primary spenders:** The first layer is hyperscaler capex, often measured as CAPEX / CFO to show how aggressively firms are reinvesting operating cash flow into AI infrastructure. Source: [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]]
  **第一层支出方：** 最核心的一层是 hyperscaler 的资本开支，常用 CAPEX / CFO 衡量其将经营现金流再投资到 AI 基础设施的力度。来源：[[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]]
- **Balance-sheet transmission:** If spending rises faster than internally generated cash, leverage and financing needs become the next constraint, making net debt / equity an important stress indicator.
  **资产负债表传导：** 如果资本开支增长快于内部现金生成能力，那么杠杆和融资需求就会成为下一重约束，因此净负债 / 权益是重要的压力指标。
- **Second-order beneficiaries:** Data-center builders, memory suppliers, energy providers, and network vendors can absorb part of the value created by the spending wave.
  **第二层受益者：** 数据中心建设者、存储供应商、能源提供方和网络设备商都会承接这轮支出创造的部分价值。
- **Earnings bridge:** Strategy notes increasingly map AI infrastructure spending to index-level earnings contribution, linking capex not just to cost pressure but also to revenue and profit tailwinds elsewhere.
  **盈利传导桥：** 越来越多的策略报告开始把 AI 基础设施支出映射到指数层面的盈利贡献，即把它从“成本压力”扩展为“也会在别处形成收入和利润顺风”。

## ⚔️ Contradictions & Evolution
- The historical contradiction is that infrastructure owners often bear the capex burden first, while later application-layer or downstream users capture the highest returns.
  历史上的核心矛盾在于：基础设施拥有者往往先承担资本开支负担，但最终获得最高回报的可能是后续应用层或下游使用者。
- In [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]], Goldman Sachs acknowledges this classic risk but argues current earnings resilience and still-low debt ratios mean the market may have moved faster than the damage.
  在 [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]] 中，高盛承认了这一经典风险，但认为当前盈利韧性和仍偏低的杠杆说明，市场价格的悲观可能走在基本面恶化之前。

## 🚀 Implementations & Best Practices
- Track CAPEX / CFO, net debt / equity, and capacity-related guidance rather than headline capex alone.
  应跟踪 CAPEX / CFO、净负债 / 权益和产能相关指引，而不是只看 headline capex。
- Distinguish between spending that expands scarce capacity and spending that merely defends competitive parity.
  要区分“扩张稀缺能力”的支出和“仅仅维持竞争平价”的支出。
- Evaluate whether downstream beneficiaries are being re-rated faster than the spenders themselves.
  应评估下游受益者的重估速度是否已经快于真正出钱的企业本身。
