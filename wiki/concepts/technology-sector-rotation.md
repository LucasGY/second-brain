# Core Definition
[[technology-sector-rotation|Technology Sector Rotation]] is the market regime in which investors reallocate capital away from, back into, or within technology depending on changing views about valuation, rates, capital intensity, competitive moats, and the relative durability of earnings.
[[technology-sector-rotation|Technology Sector Rotation]] 指的是一种市场状态：投资者会基于估值、利率、资本强度、竞争护城河以及盈利韧性的变化，在科技板块外部与内部重新配置资金。

## 🛠️ Mechanisms & Architecture
- **Relative-performance reset:** Rotation first appears in relative returns, for example when World Tech underperforms World ex-TMT or when value outperforms growth. Source: [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]]
  **相对收益重置：** 轮动最先体现在相对收益上，例如全球科技跑输全球非 TMT，或 Value 风格跑赢 Growth 风格。来源：[[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]]
- **Valuation compression and dispersion:** As confidence in a single dominant basket falls, correlations drop and investors differentiate between winners, enablers, and disrupted incumbents.
  **估值压缩与分化：** 当市场不再相信“一个龙头篮子会一直赢”时，相关性下降，投资者开始区分最终赢家、基础设施受益者以及被颠覆的旧龙头。
- **Rate and duration channel:** Technology often behaves like long-duration equity, so inflation shocks and real-rate moves can accelerate or reverse rotation.
  **利率与久期通道：** 科技股常常表现为长久期权益资产，因此通胀冲击和实际利率变化会放大或逆转轮动。
- **Capex spillover:** When AI spending shifts from software narratives toward data centers, chips, and power, part of the equity premium migrates to industrial, utility, energy, and hardware beneficiaries.
  **资本开支外溢：** 当 AI 叙事从软件转向数据中心、芯片和电力时，部分估值溢价会转移给工业、公用事业、能源和硬件受益者。

## ⚔️ Contradictions & Evolution
- A technology rotation can mean either healthy market broadening or a durable deterioration in tech economics; distinguishing the two requires checking whether earnings revisions remain resilient.
  科技轮动既可能代表市场健康扩散，也可能意味着科技经济学的长期恶化；要区分两者，关键在于检查盈利修正是否仍保持韧性。
- In [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]], Goldman Sachs argues the current episode is more valuation reset than earnings collapse, but that interpretation could fail if AI capex returns disappoint.
  在 [[20260418_manual_gs_technology_value_opportunity|GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity]] 中，高盛认为当前轮动更像估值重置而非盈利塌陷；但如果 AI 资本开支回报不及预期，这一解释就可能失效。

## 🚀 Implementations & Best Practices
- Track rotation with both price metrics and fundamental metrics; price alone cannot tell whether the move is overshoot or regime change.
  跟踪轮动时应同时看价格指标和基本面指标，因为只看价格无法判断它是超调还是范式切换。
- Separate hyperscalers, software, semiconductors, and old-economy AI enablers instead of treating technology as one homogeneous block.
  应将 hyperscaler、软件、半导体以及旧经济 AI 基建受益者分开分析，而不是把科技视为一个同质化板块。
- Use rotation analysis to size exposure changes gradually rather than to call an exact bottom.
  更适合用轮动分析来逐步调整仓位，而不是去精准判断绝对底部。
