# Core Definition
[[Parallel Path Experimentation]] is a management and product strategy in which an organization intentionally runs several plausible approaches in parallel when uncertainty is high, lead times are long, and single-path optimization would create strategic delay.
[[Parallel Path Experimentation]] 是一种管理与产品策略：当不确定性高、前置周期长、且单一路径优化会造成战略拖延时，组织有意识地并行推进多种可行方案。

## 🛠️ Mechanisms & Architecture
- Parallel paths are most justified when the options are partially complementary rather than purely redundant.
  当多条路径并非完全重复、而是部分互补时，并行推进最有价值。
- The approach treats time-to-learning as a core constraint; running nothing while debating the cleanest option is itself a strategic failure mode.
  这种方法把“获得学习反馈的时间”视为核心约束；如果为了争论最整洁的方案而什么都不做，本身就是一种战略失误。
- Shared infrastructure can improve the economics of parallel bets by letting one path de-risk or accelerate another.
  共享基础设施可以改善并行下注的经济性，因为一条路径可以为另一条路径去风险或提速。
- Amazon's 2025 shareholder letter frames same-day fulfillment centers, drones, and ultra-fast local fulfillment as a portfolio of complementary delivery paths rather than a single winner-take-all roadmap. Source: [[Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
  亚马逊 2025 年股东信把当日达履约中心、无人机与超快本地履约视为互补的配送路径组合，而不是单一胜者通吃的路线图。来源：[[Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]

## ⚔️ Contradictions & Evolution
- This concept creates tension between strategic optionality and organizational sprawl: the upside is faster learning, but the downside is fragmented attention and weak capital discipline.
  这一概念天然存在张力：一方面它带来更快的学习和更高的战略可选性，另一方面也可能造成注意力分散与资本纪律松散。
- In the current wiki state, Amazon argues that parallelism is superior when invention cycles are multi-year and customer demand is heterogeneous; future sources should test whether this remains true under tighter capital constraints.
  在当前知识库状态下，亚马逊认为，当发明周期以多年计且客户需求异质性强时，并行优于单线推进；后续来源需要检验这一判断在更紧资本约束下是否仍然成立。

## 🚀 Implementations & Best Practices
- Use parallel paths only for strategically important outcomes where waiting carries real opportunity cost.
  只有在结果具有战略重要性、且等待会带来真实机会成本时，才应采用并行路径。
- Force explicit review mechanisms so multiple bets do not become permanent resource drift.
  必须建立明确的复盘机制，避免多条下注路径永久演化为资源漂移。
- Look for infrastructure reuse across options, as with Amazon's use of fulfillment nodes to support multiple delivery experiences. Source: [[Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
  应优先寻找不同路径之间的基础设施复用，例如亚马逊用履约节点支撑多种配送体验。来源：[[Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders]]
