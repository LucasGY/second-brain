# US CPI (Consumer Price Index)

The Consumer Price Index measures inflation. Current data shows persistent inflation above Fed targets despite rate hikes.

CPI（消费者价格指数）用于衡量通胀。当前数据显示，尽管加息，通胀仍持续高于美联储目标。

## March 2026 Data | 2026年3月数据

| Metric | Value | Expected |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Headline CPI | 3.3% | 3.4% |
| Core CPI | 2.6% | 2.7% |
| Energy Inflation | +10.9% | - |

## Key Points | 关键信息

- CPI now at highest since May 2024
- Energy inflation driven by Iran War (+10.9%)
- Fed rate cuts priced out for 2026
