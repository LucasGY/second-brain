# Knowledge Workers | 知识工作者

Workers whose primary capital is knowledge rather than physical labor. Coined and popularized by Peter Drucker.

主要资本是知识而非体力劳动的工作者。由彼得·德鲁克提出并推广。

## Key Characteristics | 关键特征

### Mobility | 流动性
- Knowledge workers are mobile assets
- They must be attracted, retained, and motivated
- "The people you want, you can never get - you must make them productive"

### Productivity | 生产率
- Managing knowledge worker productivity is the biggest challenge
- Cannot be supervised in traditional ways
- Must be self-directed

### Management Implications | 管理启示
- Traditional "labor" management doesn't apply
- Need to create environment for knowledge work
- Results-based evaluation
