# First Principles Thinking | 第一性原理

A problem-solving approach that breaks down complex problems to their fundamental, self-evident truths and builds up from there.

一种解决问题的方法，将复杂问题分解到最基本的、不言自明的真理，然后从那里建立解决方案。

## Key Concept | 核心概念

Instead of reasoning by analogy (this worked before, so we'll do it similarly), first principles thinking asks:
- What are we absolutely sure is true?
- What can we derive from first principles?

不要类比推理（以前这样成功了，所以我们用类似方法），而是问：
- 什么是我们绝对确定的真理？
- 什么可以从第一性原理推导出来？

## Famous Example | 著名案例

Elon Musk on rockets:
- "Everyone said rockets cost millions... but I asked: what are rockets made of? Aerospace-grade aluminum alloys, some titanium, copper, carbon fiber. Total: about 2% of what we pay."
