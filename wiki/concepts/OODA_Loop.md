# OODA Loop | OODA循环

OODA Loop (Observe-Orient-Decide-Act) is a decision-making cycle developed by military strategist John Boyd. Used in business and media strategy.

OODA循环（观察-调整-决策-行动）是由军事战略家约翰·博伊德开发的决策循环。用于商业和媒体策略。

## Origins | 起源
- Developed by John Boyd (fighter pilot)
- Originally for air combat

## Four Stages | 四个阶段

1. **Observe** | 观察
   - Gather information
   - Watch the environment

2. **Orient** | 调整
   - Process information
   - Build mental models

3. **Decide** | 决策
   - Choose action
   - Make decisions

4. **Act** | 行动
   - Execute decision
   - Implement

## Application | 应用

### Marc Andreessen on Media | 媒体应用
- Traditional media: 24-hour decision cycle
- Internet: Real-time cycles
- Speed causes psychological collapse in competitors
