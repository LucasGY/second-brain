# De-Dollarization | 去美元化

The declining use of the US dollar in global reserves and trade.

全球储备和贸易中美元使用量的下降。

## Current Trend | 当前趋势

### Central Bank Holdings | 央行持仓
- Dollar reserves now below gold reserves
- First time since IMF data collection (1990s)
- Indicates diversification away from USD

## Implications | 影响
- Reduced dollar hegemony
- Gold and other currencies gaining share
- Shift in global monetary system
