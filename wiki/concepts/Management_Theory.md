# Management Theory | 管理理论

The systematic study of managing organizations, pioneered by Peter Drucker and others.

管理组织的系统研究，由彼得·德鲁克等人开创。

## Core Principles | 核心原则

### 1. 成果导向 | Results Orientation
- All organizational results exist externally
- Internal operations are costs, not value

### 2. 知识工作者 | Knowledge Workers
- Human capital is mobile and must be attracted/retained
- Productivity of knowledge workers is the biggest management challenge

### 3. 机会 vs 问题 | Opportunity vs Problem
- Put best talent on opportunities, not problems
- "If you only manage problems, you can only hope to return to yesterday"

### 4. 变革代理 | Change Agent
- Organizations exist to create and exploit change
- Competition is cross-industry
