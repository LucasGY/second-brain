# HBM Memory (High Bandwidth Memory)

HBM is a high-performance memory interface for AI GPUs, enabling massive bandwidth for AI training and inference workloads.

HBM（高带宽存储器）是一种用于AI GPU的高性能内存接口，为AI训练和推理工作负载提供超大带宽。

## Key Players | 主要厂商
- [[SK_Hynix]]: Leader in HBM3, starting HBM4 production
- [[Samsung]]: Major HBM producer
- [[NVIDIA]]: Primary customer for HBM

## Market Dynamics | 市场动态

### 2026 Trends
- Unprecedented memory boom driven by AI
- SK Hynix projected to surpass MS and Google in operating profit by 2026
- All new SK Hynix capacity being absorbed by HBM demand
