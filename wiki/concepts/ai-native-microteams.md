# AI-Native Microteams

## Summary

AI-native microteams are very small human teams that coordinate a larger number
of agents, allowing them to ship software and operational work with much lower
organizational overhead.

## Key Points

- Small teams avoid the coordination drag and emotional alignment costs common
  in larger organizations.
- Agents increase individual leverage across coding, documentation, operations,
  and light analysis.
- The best unit of execution may shift from department-sized teams to highly
  opinionated builders with integrated agent workflows.
- The tradeoff is that verification, prioritization, and taste become even more
  important as execution becomes cheaper.
- The more operating knowledge is captured in harnesses and agent workflows,
  the less team scale is needed to keep a complex software system moving.
