# Media Strategy | 媒体策略

Approaches and tactics for using media channels to reach audiences and build influence.

使用媒体渠道接触受众和建立影响力的方法和策略。

## Old vs New Media | 旧媒体 vs 新媒体

### Old Media | 旧媒体
- Defensive orientation
- 8-10 channels
- Permanent messages
- 24-hour decision cycles

### New Media | 新媒体
- Offensive orientation
- "If in doubt, go for it"
- Messages can be overwritten
- Real-time cycles

## Key Principles | 关键原则

### Offense Over Defense | 进攻优于防御
- In new media, offense is always better than defense
- Build content that's interesting
- "If you make a mistake, go on 30 podcasts to clarify"

### Founder-Led Media | 创始人主导媒体
- Push founders to engage directly
- CEO as media personality
- "Ship it" - publishing as service
