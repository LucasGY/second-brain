# Core Definition
AI CapEx is capital spending on data centers, accelerators, networking, power, cooling, and custom silicon required to train and serve AI workloads.
> AI 资本开支是为训练和服务 AI 工作负载而投入的数据中心、加速器、网络、电力、冷却和定制芯片资本支出。

## 🛠️ Mechanisms & Architecture
* **Capacity buildout:** Hyperscalers spend ahead of demand to secure compute capacity for internal products, cloud customers, and model-serving workloads. Source: [[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|Decoding the Agentic Economy]]
  > **产能建设：** Hyperscaler 会提前投入，以保障内部产品、云客户和模型服务工作负载所需的算力产能。来源：[[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|解码 Agentic Economy]]

* **Return mechanism:** AI CapEx becomes more defensible when higher token utilization combines with falling inference cost and stable token pricing. Source: [[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|Decoding the Agentic Economy]]
  > **回报机制：** 当更高 token 利用率、推理成本下降和 token 价格稳定同时出现时，AI 资本开支更容易被证明合理。来源：[[20260505_manual_decoding-agentic-economy|解码 Agentic Economy]]

## ⚔️ Contradictions & Evolution
* **Demand visibility versus depreciation risk:** Agentic AI can improve demand visibility, but overbuilding can still pressure cash flow, depreciation, and utilization if production adoption lags.
  > **需求可见度与折旧风险：** Agentic AI 可以改善需求可见度，但如果生产环境 adoption 滞后，过度建设仍会压迫现金流、折旧和利用率。

## 🚀 Implementations & Best Practices
* **Investment monitoring:** Track backlog conversion, AI revenue growth, cloud margin, accelerator utilization, and power availability rather than CapEx dollars alone.
  > **投资监控：** 应跟踪 backlog 转化、AI 收入增长、云毛利、加速器利用率和电力可用性，而不是只看资本开支金额。
