# Strait of Hormuz

霍尔木兹海峡

## Overview

The Strait of Hormuz is one of the world's most critical maritime chokepoints, connecting the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman and the Arabian Sea. It is the primary route for global oil shipments from Middle Eastern producers.

霍尔木兹海峡是全球最关键的海上咽喉要道之一，连接波斯湾与阿曼湾和阿拉伯海。它是中东产油国向全球输送石油的主要通道。

## Strategic Importance

战略重要性

- **Oil Transit**: Approximately 20% of global oil shipments pass through this waterway
- **Gas Transit**: Significant portion of global LNG exports
- **Geopolitical Flashpoint**: The strait's importance makes it a frequent point of tension in Middle Eastern geopolitics

## Recent Events (April 2026)

近期事件（2026年4月）

- **April 11**: Iran submitted its "red line" demanding recognition of its authority over the Strait as precondition for peace talks with the US
- **April 11**: US began the process of clearing mines from the Strait of Hormuz
- **April 10-11**: Iran was unable to fully reopen the Strait because it could not locate all the mines it deployed
