# Vertical Integration | 垂直整合

A business strategy where a company controls multiple stages of production or distribution.

企业控制生产或分销多个阶段的一种商业策略。

## Key Principles | 关键原则

### 降低成本 | Cost Reduction
- Eliminates middlemen margins
- Reduces "foolishness index"

### Quality Control | 质量控制
- Direct control over all components
- Faster iteration cycles

### Musk's "Foolishness Index" | 马斯克的"白痴指数"
- If raw materials cost 2% of finished product price, 98% is markup/profit for others
- Vertical integration captures that value

## Examples | 案例
- [[SpaceX]]: Builds rockets in-house
- [[Tesla]]: Builds batteries, cars, and energy products
- Apple: Designs chips, controls supply chain
