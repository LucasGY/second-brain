# AI Equities

_AI 股票与公司叙事_


## Summary
This module is the entrypoint for AI-related public equities, sectors, and
company narratives. It should hold investable themes, company pages, and
cross-links from technical changes to market implications.

这个模块是 AI 相关上市公司、板块和公司叙事的入口页。它应当承载可投资主题、公
司页面，以及从技术变化到市场影响的交叉链接。

## Intended Scope
- Public companies directly building AI models, chips, infrastructure, or
  software platforms.
- Suppliers and enablers whose revenue is materially shaped by AI demand.
- Thematic baskets such as semiconductors, cloud, data infrastructure, and
  agent software.

- 直接构建 AI 模型、芯片、基础设施或软件平台的上市公司。
- 收入被 AI 需求显著塑造的供应商和赋能者。
- 半导体、云、数据基础设施和 agent software 等主题篮子。

## Situation Assessment
- The repo's equity layer is currently driven more by infrastructure demand and
  supply-chain bottlenecks than by application-layer software monetization.
- The strongest investable thread is still AI hardware and upstream enablers:
  chips, memory, optics, packaging, and capex-linked suppliers.
- Market noise is high, but repeated mention patterns already reveal a small
  set of names that function as demand anchors and narrative proxies.

- 这个仓库里的股票层，目前更多由基础设施需求和供应链瓶颈驱动，而不是应用层软
  件变现。
- 最强的可投资线程仍然是 AI 硬件和上游赋能者：芯片、存储、光模块、封装，以及
  与 capex 绑定的供应商。
- 市场噪音虽然很大，但重复提及模式已经暴露出少数几个起到需求锚和叙事代理作用的
  名字。

## Situation Summary
### Semiconductors and Infrastructure
- `TSMC` and `NVIDIA` are the clearest anchors for reading AI demand.
- They matter not only as companies but as summary nodes for packaging,
  compute, and supply constraint narratives.
- `SK Hynix` makes the memory layer legible as a bottleneck, not just a
  follower of GPU demand.

- `TSMC` 和 `NVIDIA` 是观察 AI 需求最清晰的两个锚点。
- 它们的重要性不只在于公司本身，还在于它们是封装、算力和供给约束叙事的汇总节
  点。
- `SK Hynix` 则让存储层也变得清晰可读，不再只是 GPU 需求的被动跟随者。

### Political and Defense Narratives
- `Palantir` appears through a different mechanism: narrative protection,
  political alignment, and defense framing.
- That makes it important as a stock-story case, even when the raw flow is more
  rhetorical than analytical.

- `Palantir` 则通过另一种机制出现：叙事保护、政治对齐和国防框架。
- 即便原始信息流更偏修辞而非分析，它依然是很重要的股票叙事样本。

### Frontier AI Spillover
- `Anthropic` appears in this module less as a listed equity and more as a
  catalyst shaping how investors think about software repricing and model
  competition.
- This is where private-model narratives can still matter for public equities.

- `Anthropic` 在这个模块里并不是因为它本身是上市公司，而是因为它会影响投资者对
  软件重定价和模型竞争的理解。
- 这说明私有模型公司的叙事，依然会外溢到公开市场。

## Top Themes
- AI equities are currently most legible through infrastructure and supply
  concentration.
- Memory and HBM are becoming first-order equity signals, not secondary details
  inside a generic semiconductor basket.
- The equity layer should increasingly be organized around demand chains and
  narrative clusters, not just isolated company mentions.
- Durable stock knowledge in this repo should come from repeated signals, not
  one-off tweet excitement.

- AI 股票目前最容易通过基础设施和供给集中度来理解。
- 存储和 HBM 正在变成一线股票信号，而不是泛半导体篮子里的二级细节。
- 这个模块以后更适合按需求链和叙事簇来组织，而不只是零散公司点名。
- 这个仓库里的持久股票知识，应来自重复信号，而不是一次性 tweet 情绪。
