# Corporate Insiders: Bullish Despite Geopolitics

## Summary | 摘要

Despite Iran War, 26.4% of US companies saw net insider purchases in March, highest in 5 months. Sign of confidence in recovery.

尽管伊朗战争，26.4%的美国公司3月出现内部人士净买入，为5个月最高。对复苏充满信心。

## Key Data | 关键数据
- March: 26.4% net insider purchases (highest in 5 months)
- February: 20.9%
- 10-year average: 23.5%
- 2nd consecutive monthly increase

## Sector Dynamics | 行业差异
- Overall: Bullish (buying stocks)
- Energy: Only 17.5% (insiders don't expect high oil prices)

## Interpretation | 解读
- Executives buying own stock after pullback
- Confidence in market recovery
- Energy insiders skeptical of price sustainability
