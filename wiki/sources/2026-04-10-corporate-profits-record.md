# Record-High Corporate Profits

## Summary | 摘要

Corporate profits at record-high levels despite elevated costs, tariffs, wages, and interest rates. Strong resilience in corporate earnings.

企业利润创历史新高，尽管成本、关税、工资和利率均处于高位。显示出强劲韧性。

## Key Points | 关键信息

### Record Profits | 创纪录利润
- Record-high despite multiple headwinds
- Cost pressures absorbed
- Pricing power maintained

### Resilience Factors | 韧性因素
- Tariffs impact manageable
- Wage pressures offset by productivity
- Interest costs absorbed
