# AI Agents: Software Eating Knowledge Work

## Summary | 摘要

Peter Yang from Roblox discusses how AI agents are reshaping software development. Key topics include vibe coding, the death of SaaS, 2-3 person teams with agents replacing 10-person teams, and AI as a path to pursuing dreams.

Roblox的Peter Yang讨论AI智能体如何重塑软件开发。关键话题包括氛围编程、SaaS的终结、2-3人团队加智能体取代10人团队，以及AI作为追求梦想的途径。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### Programming Eating Knowledge Work | 编程吞噬知识性工作
- Code is being abstracted away
- Future: just communicate with agents to get things done
- All knowledge work (docs, slides, design) becomes conversation with agents

### Personal AI Agent Zoe | 个人AI伙伴Zoe
- Named personal AI agent for deeper bonding
- 70-80% of usage driven by personalization
- Voice interaction transforms AI from tool to companion
- Can provide motivational talks during walks

### Vibe Coding | 氛围编程
- AI-native companies building internal tools with AI
- Replacing traditional SaaS subscriptions (e.g., Calendly)
- Cost-benefit of maintaining tools still debated
- Barrier to "making tools" has dropped to zero

### Small Teams > Large Teams | 小团队 > 大团队
- 2-3 person teams more efficient than 10-person teams
- Large companies inefficient due to "alignment" costs
- Alignment with agents easier than alignment with humans
- "Agents remove emotional factors"

### Agent Stack | 智能体技术栈
- Identity, payments, marketing emerging
- CLI vs MCP (Model Context Protocol)
- Everything is new in agent technology

### Dream Opportunity | 梦想机遇
- Bad job market forces people to pursue dreams
- AI tools enable individuals to build businesses
- "You lost your job, but now you can really do your own thing"
