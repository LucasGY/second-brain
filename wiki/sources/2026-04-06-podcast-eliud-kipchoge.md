# Eliud Kipchoge: Philosophy of Discipline and Breaking Limits

## Summary | 摘要

Eliud Kipchoge, the world's fastest marathon runner, shares his philosophy on discipline, mental training, and breaking human limits. Key concepts include "Vitamin N" (saying No), the power of handwritten journals, and "discipline is freedom."

马拉松世界纪录保持者基普乔格分享他对自律、心理训练和突破人类极限的哲学。关键概念包括"维生素N"（说不的力量）、手写日记的力量，以及"自律即自由"。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### Success Formula | 成功公式
- "I don't believe in success itself, I believe in pure and sufficient preparation"
- "Pure and clear planning leads to success"
- One step at a time, one sleep at a time, one run at a time

### Climbing Tree Philosophy | 爬树哲学
- Breaking a record is like climbing a tree
- After grabbing one branch, immediately target the next
- Once secure, forget the past - can't move up looking back

### Vitamin N: Discipline Foundation | 维生素N：自律基石
- **Rule 1**: Inject "Vitamin N" (Learn to say No)
- **Rule 2**: Set priorities correctly
- **Rule 3**: Avoid complaining
- Discipline is not a sprint, it's like fitness - takes months/years

### Power of Handwritten Journals | 手写日记的力量
- 90 handwritten journals since 2003
- Records every training session, every shoe, every ice bath
- Writing means remembering
- Desire to fill journals prevents missing training

### Kipchoge Smile | 基普乔格微笑
- Pain is part of success
- Pain is a compass respecting achievement
- When pain reaches level 10, those who learn to embrace and move forward with pain cross the finish line

### Discipline = Freedom | 自律即自由
- "Disciplined people are free in life"
- "Undisciplined people are not free"
- "They are like in prison"
- Discipline is the first rule of self-mastery

### Team and Social Motivation | 团队与社交动力
- Never trains alone despite being #1
- Team protects thoughts from wandering
- Running as "family lifestyle"
- "If humans move, the world becomes better"
