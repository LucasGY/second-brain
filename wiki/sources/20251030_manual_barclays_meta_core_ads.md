# Barclays: Core Ads Ripping, But So Is Opex & Capex

## 📌 TL;DR
Barclays maintains a positive view on Meta's core advertising strength but flags that operating expense and capital expenditure growth are keeping pace with revenue gains, limiting margin expansion potential.
Barclays 对 Meta 核心广告实力保持乐观，但指出运营支出和资本支出增长与收入增长持平，限制了利润率扩张潜力。

---

## 🎯 Core Investment Proposition
核心广告业务的强势能否抵消成本膨胀对利润率的影响？
Can strong core advertising offset cost inflation's impact on margins?

## 🔍 Critical Breakdown

* **Question/Argument:** Core ads business remains robust
  * **Their Evidence:** Family Apps revenue growing; ad load stable; pricing power intact
  * **Evidence Quality:** Hard data supports bull case; macro risk exists
  * **Missing Variables:** Market share dynamics with TikTok
  * **Independent Assessment:** Core business is fine; not exceptional

* **Question/Argument:** Opex growth mirrors revenue growth
  * **Their Evidence:** AI hiring, infrastructure costs keeping expense ratio stable
  * **Evidence Quality:** Expense discipline appears reasonable for investment phase
  * **Missing Variables:** Break-even point for efficiency gains
  * **Independent Assessment:** Opex trend is manageable if AI delivers

* **Question/Argument:** Capex is the wild card for FCF
  * **Their Evidence:** Capex guidance elevated; FCF will be under pressure
  * **Evidence Quality:** Credible given competitive requirements
  * **Missing Variables:** Timeline for FCF recovery
  * **Independent Assessment:** Capex risk is real but appears necessary

## 👻 Implicit Assumptions

* AI investments will eventually yield margin expansion
* Core ad business can sustain growth through competitive pressures
* No major regulatory disruption to business model

## ⚡ Alpha & Expectation Gap

* **Market Consensus (Priced In):** Constructive on revenue but cautious on margins
* **Marginal Information:** Balanced view of both bull and bear cases for cost structure
