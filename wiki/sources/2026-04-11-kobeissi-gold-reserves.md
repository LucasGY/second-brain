# Kobeissi Letter: Gold Reshaping Global Financial System

## Summary | 摘要

Central bank gold holdings have surpassed valuation-adjusted US Dollar reserve assets. Dollar-denominated reserves now represent only 46% of global FX and gold reserves.

央行黄金持有量已超过经估值调整后的美元储备资产。美元计价储备现在仅占全球外汇和黄金储备的46%。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Gold Reserves | 黄金储备
- **Central bank gold**: Surpassed USD reserves | 超过美元储备
- **Dollar share**: ~46% of global reserves | 约占全球储备46%
- **Trend**: Lowest level since... | 创历史新低

### Dollar Decline | 美元下跌
- De-dollarization accelerating | 去美元化加速
- Central banks diversifying | 央行分散投资
- Gold as reserve asset rising | 黄金作为储备资产上升

## Implications | 影响
- Dollar reserve status challenged | 美元储备地位受挑战
- Gold prices supported | 金价获支撑
- Multipolar currency system emerging | 多极货币体系出现
