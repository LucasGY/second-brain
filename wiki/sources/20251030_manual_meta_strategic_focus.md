## 🔍 Strategic Focus Areas

### 1. AI-Powered Ads (AI 驱动广告)
* Meta frames AI ad tools as a sustainable competitive moat, not just incremental improvement
* Advantage+ automation reduces advertiser friction while improving targeting precision
* Meta 将 AI 广告工具定位为可持续的竞争护城河，而非仅仅是增量改进
* Advantage+ 自动化降低了广告商摩擦，同时提高了定位精度

### 2. Llama Ecosystem (Llama 生态系统)
* Open-source strategy aims to shape industry standards and developer adoption
* Enterprise partnerships expanding; Llama becoming infrastructure choice
* 开源战略旨在塑造行业标准和开发者采纳
* 企业合作扩大；Llama 正在成为基础设施选择

### 3. Llama AI Use Cases (Llama AI 用例)
* Meta AI assistant integrated across Family of Apps
* Multimodal capabilities (image generation, video understanding) in development
* Meta AI 助手集成到 Family of Apps 中
* 多模态能力（图像生成、视频理解）正在开发中

### 4. Reality Labs Innovation (Reality Labs 创新)
* Long-term bet on AR/VR as next computing platform
*ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses adoption exceeding expectations
* 对 AR/VR 作为下一代计算平台的长期押注
* Ray-Ban Meta 智能眼镜采用率超出预期

### 5. Organizational Resilience (组织韧性)
* Focus on operational efficiency despite investment mode
* Workforce restructuring complete; leaner cost structure
* 尽管处于投资模式，仍注重运营效率
* 劳动力重组完成；更精简的成本结构

## 👻 Implicit Assumptions

* AI advertising moat will hold against Google, Amazon, TikTok competition
* Llama ecosystem will generate strategic rather than direct financial returns
* Reality Labs will eventually contribute meaningfully to revenue
