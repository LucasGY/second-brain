# Kobeissi Letter: E-Commerce at Record 16.6% of Retail

## Summary | 摘要

E-commerce in the US has reached a record 16.6% of total US retail sales, showing continued shift to online shopping despite economic headwinds.

美国电子商务已占美国零售总额的创纪录16.6%，显示出尽管面临经济逆风，在线购物仍在持续转移。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### E-Commerce Share | 电子商务份额
- **Current**: 16.6% of retail | 占零售16.6%
- **Record high**: All-time high | 创历史新高
- **Trend**: Steady increase | 持续增长

### Implications | 影响
- Traditional retail under pressure | 传统零售承压
- Logistics and fulfillment booming | 物流和履约蓬勃发展
- Consumer behavior shift permanent | 消费者行为转变永久化
