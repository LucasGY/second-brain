# Google: AI Mode Restaurant Booking Expansion

## Summary | 摘要

Google expands AI Mode agentic restaurant booking to 8 new countries. AI Mode searches across platforms for real-time availability and completes bookings through partners.

谷歌将AI Mode智能餐厅预订扩展到8个新国家。AI Mode跨平台搜索实时可用性并通过合作伙伴完成预订。

## Key Features | 关键功能

### Agentic Booking | 智能预订
- **Natural language**: Describe what you want | 用自然语言描述需求
- **Multi-platform search**: Real-time availability | 跨平台搜索实时可用性
- **One-tap booking**: Through partner integration | 一键通过合作伙伴预订

### Expansion | 扩展
- **First time outside US**: Global expansion | 首次美国以外扩展
- **New markets**: 8 countries | 8个新国家
- **Coverage**: Australia, Canada, HK, India, NZ, Singapore, SA, UK | 澳大利亚、加拿大、香港、印度、新西兰、新加坡、南非、英国
