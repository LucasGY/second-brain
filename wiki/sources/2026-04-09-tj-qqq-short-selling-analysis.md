# TJ Research: QQQ Short Selling Strategy Analysis

## Summary | 摘要

Analysis of QQQ short selling: QQQ rebounded ~10% from bottom, even someone fully shorted on March 10 is only +0.4%. Discussion on why short sellers often cut positions at bottom, and why adding on the way down and up is key.

QQQ做空分析：QQQ从底部反弹近10%，即使有人在3月10日满仓做空，到现在也只有+0.4%。讨论为何做空者经常在底部止损，以及为何在下跌和上涨时加仓是关键。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### QQQ Performance | QQQ表现
- **Bottom rebound**: +10% from lows | 底部反弹：从低点上涨10%
- **Short position**: Full short on March 10 only +0.4% | 做空头寸：3月10日满仓做空仅+0.4%
- **Context**: Market recovery despite Iran War | 背景：尽管伊朗战争市场仍复苏

### Short Selling Paradox | 做空悖论
- **Left side**: Adding short on way down | 左侧：下跌时加空
- **Right side**: Adding short on way up | 右侧：上涨时加空
- **Result**: Both sides short = break-even or small profit | 结果：两边做空=盈亏平衡或小幅盈利

### Psychology of Shorting | 做空心理
- **Cutting at bottom**: Emotional response to losses | 底部止损：对亏损的情绪反应
- **Not adding on recovery**: Fear of further losses | 复苏时不加仓：害怕进一步亏损
- **Meat cleaver effect**: Cutting positions at worst time | 剁肉效应：在最糟糕时机止损

### Trading Wisdom | 交易智慧
- **Left side add**: Adding on decline can be profitable | 左侧加仓：下跌时加仓可以盈利
- **Right side add**: Adding on recovery compounds gains | 右侧加仓：复苏时加仓复利
- **Reality**: Most can't execute this psychologically | 现实：大多数人在心理上无法执行
