# Thomas Wolf: Small Models Can Detect Mythos Vulnerabilities

## Summary | 摘要

Hugging Face tests show small open-weight models can detect vulnerabilities similar to Anthropic's Mythos. 8 out of 8 models detected Mythos's flagship exploit, including 3.6B parameter models at $0.11/M tokens.

Hugging Face测试显示小型开源模型可以检测类似Anthropic Mythos的漏洞。8个模型中有8个检测到Mythos的旗舰漏洞，包括仅36亿参数、成本0.11美元/百万token的模型。

## Key Findings | 关键发现

### Vulnerability Detection | 漏洞检测
- **Mythos exploit**: Detected by all 8 tested models | 被所有8个测试模型检测到
- **Small models**: Even 3.6B parameters effective | 甚至36亿参数模型也有效
- **Cost**: $0.11 per million tokens | 成本：每百万token 0.11美元

### Open Source vs Closed | 开源与闭源
- Open models recover similar analysis | 开源模型恢复类似分析
- Small models competitive with flagship | 小模型与旗舰模型竞争
- Security democratization | 安全民主化
