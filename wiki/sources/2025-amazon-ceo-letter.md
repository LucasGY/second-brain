# Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders

## Summary | 摘要

Andy Jassy's comprehensive 2025 letter to shareholders covering Amazon's AI strategy, AWS growth, robotics, and key lessons on managing major business inflections.

Andy Jassy 2025年致股东信，涵盖亚马逊AI战略、AWS增长、机器人和管理重大业务转折点的关键经验。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### AI is the Biggest Inflection | AI是最大转折点
- Every customer experience will be reinvented by AI | 每个客户体验都将被AI重塑
- AI adoption faster than any previous technology | AI采用速度快于任何先前技术
- AWS AI revenue: $15B+ run rate in Q1 2026 | AWS AI营收：2026年Q1超过150亿美元

### AWS Strategy | AWS战略
- Trainium chips: 30-40% better price-performance | Trainium芯片：性价比提升30-40%
- Custom silicon strategy working | 定制硅策略奏效
- Capex investment: ~$200B in 2026 | 资本支出：2026年约2000亿美元

### Key Lessons | 关键经验
- "2 > 0": Pursue parallel paths | 追求多条平行路径
- Bet big on disproportionate inflections | 在重大转折点上大胆下注
- Accept going back to the starting line | 接受回到起点重新开始
- Cultivate culture for squiggly lines | 培养应对曲折道路的文化

## Financial Highlights | 财务亮点
- **2025 Revenue**: $717B (+12% YoY) | 营收：7170亿美元（同比增长12%）
- **AWS Revenue**: $129B (+20% YoY) | AWS营收：1290亿美元（同比增长20%）
- **Operating Income**: $80B (11.2% margin) | 营业利润：800亿美元（利润率11.2%）

## Quotes | 引述
> "AI may have comparable impact [to electricity]. The difference is that electricity took 40 years to get where it was going. AI appears to be moving ten times faster."
> AI可能具有可与电力相比的影响。区别在于电力花了40年才达到目标。AI的移动速度似乎快了10倍。
