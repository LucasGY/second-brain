# Anthropic 系统提示词限制更新

## Summary | 摘要

Anthropic's system prompt restrictions are evolving. Third-party apps now draw from extra usage rather than user's plan limits, with a $100 credit provided.

Anthropic 的系统提示词限制政策持续演变。第三方应用现在从额外用量中扣费，而非用户的套餐限额，并提供 $100 信用额度。

## Key Points | 关键信息

- Anthropic blocking third-party access to system prompts
- Third-party apps now use separate usage quota
- $100 credit provided for new users
