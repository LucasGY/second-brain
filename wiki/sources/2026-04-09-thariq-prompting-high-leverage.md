# Thariq: Prompting is High-Leverage Skill Like Writing

## Summary | 摘要

Thariq argues that "prompting" will remain an incredibly high-leverage skill, comparable to writing or public speaking. It is the skill of effectively communicating with agents through their harnesses.

Thariq认为"提示工程"将继续是一个极高杠杆的技能，类似于写作或公开演讲。这是通过智能体的"挽具"(harness)与智能体有效沟通的技能。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Prompting as Core Skill | 作为核心技能的提示工程
- **Comparison**: Writing and public speaking | 类比：写作和公开演讲
- **Nature**: Skill of talking to agents | 本质：与智能体对话的技能
- **Mechanism**: Mediated by the harness | 机制：通过harness中介

### Thariq's Mission | Thariq的使命
- **Goal**: Grow bandwidth between humans and agents | 目标：增加人类与智能体之间的带宽
- **Purpose**: Help us understand each other better | 目的：帮助我们更好地相互理解
- **Focus**: Human-AI communication | 焦点：人机沟通

### Implications | 影响

#### Skill Development | 技能发展
- Prompting becoming essential literacy | 提示工程成为必备素养
- Like writing was in the digital age | 像数字时代的写作一样重要
- Evolving with AI capabilities | 随AI能力演进

#### Career Impact | 职业影响
- High leverage for productivity | 对生产力高度杠杆化
- Differentiates effective users | 区分有效用户
- Foundational for AI-native work | AI原生工作的基础
