# Historic US Market Recovery

## Summary | 摘要

US stock market seeing historic recovery with S&P 500 up 7 consecutive sessions. Recovered nearly entire war decline.

美国股市创历史性复苏，标普500连续7个交易日上涨，收复了几乎全部战争跌幅。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Streak | 连涨
- S&P 500: 7 consecutive green days
- Longest since October 2025
- Nasdaq: Similar 7-day streak

### Gains | 涨幅
- S&P 500: +7.6% during streak
- Nearly all war decline recovered

### Historical Pattern | 历史规律
- Similar streaks happened only 9 times since 1950s
- 8/9 times higher next month (+4.4% avg)
- 7/9 times higher next 3 months (+10.2% avg)

## Market Outlook | 市场展望
- History says momentum set to continue
- Short covering and recovery combining
