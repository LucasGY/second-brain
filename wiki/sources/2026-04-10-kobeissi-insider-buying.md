# Kobeissi: Insiders Not Bearish Despite Iran War

## Summary | 摘要

Despite the Iran War, corporate insiders remain bullish on their stocks. 26.4% of US publicly traded companies saw net insider purchases in March 2026, the highest in 5 months and above the 10-year average of 23.5%.

尽管伊朗战争持续，企业内部人士仍对自家股票保持乐观。2026年3月，26.4%的美国上市公司出现内部人士净买入，为5个月来最高，且高于23.5%的10年平均水平。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Insider Buying Surge | 内部人士买入激增
- **March 2026**: 26.4% of companies with net insider purchases | 2026年3月：26.4%的公司有内部人士净买入
- **February 2026**: 20.9% (prior month) | 2026年2月：20.9%（上月）
- **10-year average**: 23.5% | 10年平均：23.5%
- **Trend**: 2nd consecutive monthly increase | 趋势：连续第2个月增加

### Energy Sector Divergence | 能源板块分化
- **Energy**: 17.5% with net purchases, down -1.6pp | 能源：17.5%净买入，下降1.6个百分点
- **Interpretation**: Insiders don't expect oil prices to stay elevated | 解读：内部人士预期油价不会持续高企

### Market Signal | 市场信号
- Corporate executives stepped in after market pullback | 高管在市场回调后入场买入
- Sign of confidence in recovery | 对复苏的信心信号
- Bullish positioning despite geopolitical uncertainty | 尽管地缘政治不确定仍保持看涨
