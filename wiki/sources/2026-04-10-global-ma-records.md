# Global M&A at Record: Q1 2026

## Summary | 摘要

Global M&A transaction values jumped +18% YoY to $1.25 trillion in Q1 2026, the largest Q1 total on record. On track to surpass $4.5 trillion for full year.

2026年Q1全球并购交易额同比增长18%达1.25万亿美元，创Q1历史记录。全年有望超过4.5万亿美元。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Q1 2026 | Q1 2026
- Total: $1.25 trillion
- YoY growth: +18%
- Largest Q1 on record

### Notable Deals | 重大交易
- Unilever food business: $44.8B to McCormick
- Sysco/Jetro: $29.1B deal

### Historical Context | 历史背景
- +$670B in 3 years (+116%)
- 18 deals worth $10B+ in one quarter
- Full year on track for ~$4.5T (record)

## Market Implications | 市场影响
- Corporate confidence high
- Strategic M&A activity robust
- Valuations supporting deals
