# Chinese Robotics Competition: Unitree vs Dreame Talent War

## Summary | 摘要

Competition among Chinese robotics companies is reaching unprecedented intensity. Dreame CEO reportedly offered RMB 200 million ($30 million) annual salary to poach Unitree's chief scientist.

中国机器人公司之间的竞争达到前所未有的激烈程度。Dreame CEO据报道向Unitree首席科学家开出了2亿元人民币（3000万美元）的年薪。

- **Offer**: RMB 200 million/year (~$30 million USD)
- **Target**: Unitree chief scientist
- **Context**: Intense competition in Chinese robotics sector
