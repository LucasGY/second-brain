# Paulo: Unitree vs Dreame - $30M Salary to Poach Chief Scientist

## Summary | 摘要

Chinese robotics companies are in intense talent war. Dreame CEO reportedly instructed team to poach Unitree's chief scientist even at RMB 200 million (~$30 million) annual salary, highlighting extreme competition in robotics sector.

中国机器人公司正在激烈的人才战争中。追觅CEO据说指示团队以2亿人民币（约3000万美元）的年薪挖角宇树科技首席科学家，凸显机器人领域的极端竞争。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Talent War | 人才争夺战
- **Competition**: Beyond intense among Chinese robotics firms | 竞争：中国机器人公司之间竞争极为激烈
- **Offer**: RMB 200 million (~$30M) annual salary | 出价：2亿人民币（约3000万美元）年薪
- **Target**: Unitree's chief scientist | 目标：宇树科技首席科学家

### Industry Context | 行业背景
- **Dreame**: Premium home robot vacuum maker | 追觅：高端家用扫地机器人制造商
- **Unitree**: Leading quadruped and humanoid robot maker | 宇树科技：领先的四足和人形机器人制造商
- **Yu Hao**: Founder and CEO of Dreame | 追觅创始人兼CEO

### Market Implications | 市场影响
- Robotics talent at premium | 机器人人才价值飙升
- Chinese tech investing heavily in automation | 中国科技大力投资自动化
- Global robotics competition intensifying | 全球机器人竞争加剧
