# QQQ Short Selling Analysis: Market Timing Strategy

## Summary | 摘要

Analysis of QQQ short selling strategy. Despite QQQ rebounding nearly 10% from bottom, someone who was fully shorted on March 10 would still be up 0.4%. Discussion of timing traps in short selling.

QQQ做空策略分析。尽管QQQ从底部反弹近10%，但3月10日满仓做空的人仍能盈利0.4%。讨论做空时机陷阱。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### Market Dynamics | 市场动态
- QQQ bottom rebound: ~10%
- March 10 full short: still +0.4% profit
- Short sellers face timing uncertainty

### Timing Trap | 时机陷阱
- Left side (下降): Small short position = profit
- Right side (上涨): Small short position = loss
- Most end up breaking even or small profit
- Real winners: Left + right side additions

### Psychological Trap | 心理陷阱
- Cut positions at bottom when dropping
- Don't add when rebounding
- Some retail gets stopped out at bottom
