# Paulo: SK Hynix M15X Adds +50K Wafers/Month, All Absorbed by HBM

## Summary | 摘要

Paulo highlights that SK Hynix's DRAM production increase from M15X fab adds +50,000 wafers/month, all of which will be absorbed by HBM demand. The new capacity is immediately taken up by high-bandwidth memory requirements.

Paulo强调SK海力士M15X晶圆厂的DRAM增产将增加50,000片/月，全部被HBM需求吸收。新增产能立即被高带宽存储器需求所消耗。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### M15X Capacity Addition | M15X产能增加
- **Additional output**: +50,000 wafers/month | 新增产出：+50,000片/月
- **Destination**: All absorbed by HBM | 去向：全部被HBM吸收
- **Context**: SK Hynix ramping HBM4 production | 背景：SK海力士扩大HBM4生产

### HBM Demand Absorption | HBM需求吸收
- **Full utilization**: No excess capacity created | 满负荷使用：未产生过剩产能
- **Structural demand**: AI infrastructure consuming supply | 结构需求：AI基础设施消耗供应
- **Implication**: DRAM prices supported | 影响：DRAM价格获得支撑

### Supply-Demand Dynamics | 供需动态
- **Demand source**: AI servers, data centers | 需求来源：AI服务器、数据中心
- **Supply response**: Capacity expansion immediately absorbed | 供应响应：产能扩张立即被吸收
- **Outlook**: Sustained tightness expected | 展望：预期持续紧张
