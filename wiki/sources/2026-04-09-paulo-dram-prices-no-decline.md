# Paulo: Only Samsung and CXMT Can Scale DRAM Production, Prices Won't Fall

## Summary | 摘要

Paulo argues that only Samsung and CXMT (ChangXin Memory Technologies) can scale DRAM production significantly, and both are busy with HBM. DRAM prices won't fall as long as AI data centers consume DRAM supply.

Paulo认为只有三星和长鑫存储（CXMT）能大规模扩大DRAM产能，而且两者都忙于HBM。只要AI数据中心继续消耗DRAM供应，DRAM价格就不会下跌。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### DRAM Supply Constraint | DRAM供应限制
- **Scalable producers**: Only Samsung and CXMT | 可规模化的生产商：只有三星和长鑫存储
- **HBM priority**: Both busy with HBM production | HBM优先：两者都忙于HBM生产
- **Supply/demand**: AI demand absorbing all supply | 供需：AI需求吸收全部供应

### AI Data Center Demand | AI数据中心需求
- **Consumption**: AI data centers consuming DRAM supply | 消耗：AI数据中心消耗DRAM供应
- **Duration**: No relief until demand eases | 持续：直到需求缓解才有缓解
- **Implication**: Structural demand shift | 影响：结构性需求转变

### Pricing Outlook | 定价展望
- **Bullish**: DRAM prices won't fall | 看涨：DRAM价格不会下跌
- **Duration**: Sustained elevated pricing | 持续：持续的高价位
- **Winners**: HBM-focused memory makers | 赢家：专注HBM的存储器厂商
