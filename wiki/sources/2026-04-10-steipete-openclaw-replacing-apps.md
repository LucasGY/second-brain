# Steipete: OpenClaw Replacing Apps

## Summary | 摘要

Reda Sadki shares that they're using fewer and fewer apps, more and more OpenClaw. Single AI tool replacing multiple applications.

Reda Sadki分享他们使用的应用越来越少，OpenClaw越来越多。单一AI工具正在取代多个应用程序。

## Key Observation | 关键观察

### App Consolidation | 应用整合
- **Fewer apps**: Using less traditional software | 使用更少传统软件
- **OpenClaw focus**: AI as primary tool | AI作为主要工具
- **CLI occasional**: Just command line occasionally | 偶尔只用命令行

### Future of Software | 软件未来
- AI replacing apps | AI取代应用
- Consolidation trend | 整合趋势
- Single interface for work | 单一工作界面
