# Kobeissi Letter: Private Credit Redemptions Hit Record

## Summary | 摘要

Cracks in the US private credit market are widening. Investors requested a record $14.0 billion in redemptions from private credit funds in March 2026.

美国私人信贷市场的裂缝正在扩大。投资者在2026年3月从私人信贷基金请求创纪录的140亿美元赎回。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Redemptions | 赎回
- **March 2026**: $14.0 billion | 140亿美元
- **Record high**: Largest single month | 创单月最高
- **Trend**: Widening cracks | 裂缝扩大

### Market Concerns | 市场担忧
- Iran War impact on credit markets | 伊朗战争对信贷市场影响
- Liquidity concerns rising | 流动性担忧上升
- Risk-off sentiment increasing | 风险规避情绪增强
