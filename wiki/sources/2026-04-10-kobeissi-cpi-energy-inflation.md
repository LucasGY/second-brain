# Kobeissi Letter: March 2026 CPI and Energy Inflation Data

## Summary | 摘要

March 2026 US CPI and energy inflation data from The Kobeissi Letter. Key findings include largest monthly gas price jump since 1967 and energy inflation surge of +10.9%.

2026年3月美国CPI和能源通胀数据。关键发现包括自1967年以来最大的月度汽油价格上涨，以及能源通胀飙升+10.9%。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### CPI Data | CPI数据
- **Headline CPI**: 3.3% (below 3.4% expected)
- **Core CPI**: 2.6% (below 2.7% expected)
- **Highest since**: May 2024 amid Iran War
- **Fed rate cuts**: Priced out for 2026

### Energy Inflation | 能源通胀
- **March 2026 surge**: +10.9%
- **Context**: Iran War impact
- **Largest monthly jump since**: 2005
- **Gas prices**: Largest monthly jump in CPI data since 1967

## Implications | 影响
- CPI below expectations = inflation not as bad as feared
- But highest level since May 2024
- Energy prices significantly impacted by Iran War
- Fed rate cuts unlikely in 2026
