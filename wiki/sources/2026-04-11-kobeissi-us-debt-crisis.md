# Kobeissi Letter: US Debt Crisis - 18 Cents per Dollar

## Summary | 摘要

The US government spent 18 cents of every dollar of revenue on interest payments alone in early FY2026. The first 6 months produced a deficit of $1.17 trillion.

美国政府在2026财年初每花费一美元收入中就有18美分仅用于支付利息。前六个月产生1.17万亿美元的赤字。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Debt Metrics | 债务指标
- **Interest spending**: 18 cents per dollar | 每美元18美分利息
- **FY2026 deficit (H1)**: $1.17 trillion | 1.17万亿美元
- **Trajectory**: Uncharted territory | 进入未知领域

### Context | 背景
- Interest payments consuming record share of revenue | 利息支付占收入创纪录比例
- Deficit spending accelerating | 赤字支出加速
- Debt ceiling concerns mounting | 债务上限担忧加剧

## Implications | 影响
- Fed rate cuts constrained | 美联储降息受限
- Dollar reserve status pressured | 美元储备地位承压
- De-dollarization accelerates | 去美元化加速
