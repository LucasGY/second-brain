# Paulo: Japan's Lost Generation - Yen Savings Crisis

## Summary | 摘要

Paulo comments on Japan's "lost generation" (氷河期世代) crisis: most survivors only save in yen, which is truly cruel. Questions whether 30 million yen at age 70 will be enough, especially for home repairs.

Paulo评论日本"迷失一代"（氷河期世代）危机：大多数幸存者只存日元，这真的很残酷。质疑70岁时3000万日元是否足够，尤其是房屋修缮。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Lost Generation Crisis | 迷失一代危机
- **Generation**: 氷河期世代 (Lost Generation) | 代际：迷失一代
- **Savings behavior**: Only saving in yen | 储蓄行为：只存日元
- **Social issue**: Truly cruel situation | 社会问题：真正残酷的处境

### Financial Reality | 财务现实
- **Target amount**: 30 million yen at 70 | 目标金额：70岁时3000万日元
- **Question**: Will it be enough? | 问题：这够吗？
- **Risk**: Home repairs could consume half | 风险：房屋修缮可能消耗一半

### Implications | 影响
- **Demographics**: Aging population challenge | 人口统计：老龄化人口挑战
- **Currency risk**: Yen holdings vulnerable | 货币风险：日元持仓脆弱
- **Social safety net**: Questions about retirement security | 社会安全网：退休保障问题
