# Google Gemini: Lyria 3 Pro - Longer Tracks for Free

## Summary | 摘要

Google Gemini rolls out longer tracks with Lyria 3 Pro for free. Users can select "Create music" in tools menu and "Thinking" or "Pro" from model picker to generate longer music with complex transitions.

Google Gemini推出Lyria 3 Pro的更长曲目免费功能。用户可在工具菜单中选择"Create music"，从模型选择器中选择"Thinking"或"Pro"来生成带有复杂过渡的更长音乐。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Lyria 3 Pro Features | Lyria 3 Pro功能
- **Longer tracks**: Generate longer music | 更长曲目：生成更长的音乐
- **Complex transitions**: Tracks with complex transitions | 复杂过渡：带有复杂过渡的曲目
- **Free access**: Available for free to users | 免费访问：用户可免费使用

### Access Method | 访问方式
- **Menu**: Select "Create music" in tools menu | 菜单：在工具菜单中选择"Create music"
- **Model picker**: Choose "Thinking" or "Pro" | 模型选择器：选择"Thinking"或"Pro"
- **Platforms**: Google AI Plus, Pro, Ultra users | 平台：Google AI Plus、Pro、Ultra用户

### Music Generation | 音乐生成
- **Style experiments**: Different music styles | 风格实验：不同的音乐风格
- **Vision**: Bring full vision to life | 愿景：将完整愿景变为现实
- **Sharing**: Encouraging user creations | 分享：鼓励用户创作
