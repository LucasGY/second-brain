# Paulo: SK Hynix M15X Fab Begins Full-Scale HBM4 Production

## Summary | 摘要

SK Hynix begins full-scale HBM4 DRAM production at new M15X fab in Cheongju. The fab will ramp from 10,000 wafers/month this month to 80,000 wafers/month by next year, serving as the primary facility for next-generation HBM demand.

SK海力士在忠清的新M15X晶圆厂开始全面生产HBM4 DRAM。该工厂本月将从10,000片/月起步，目标明年达到80,000片/月，成为下一代HBM需求的主要生产设施。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### M15X Fab Details | M15X晶圆厂详情
- **Location**: Cheongju, adjacent to existing M15 | 位置：忠清，与现有M15相邻
- **Investment**: ~KRW 20 trillion | 投资：约20万亿韩元
- **EUV equipment**: ~KRW 12 trillion worth deployed | EUV设备：部署约12万亿韩元

### Production Ramp | 产能爬坡
- **April 2026**: 10,000 wafers/month initial | 2026年4月：初始10,000片/月
- **End 2026**: 30,000-50,000 wafers/month | 2026年底：30,000-50,000片/月
- **2027**: Up to 80,000 wafers/month | 2027年：高达80,000片/月
- **Timeline**: ~2 months ahead of schedule | 时间表：比原计划提前约2个月

### HBM4 Allocation | HBM4分配
- **Primary**: 6th-gen HBM (HBM4) | 主要：第6代HBM (HBM4)
- **Secondary**: High-performance server DRAM | 次要：高性能服务器DRAM
- **Impact**: +10-15% total DRAM output | 影响：总DRAM产出增加10-15%

### Strategic Importance | 战略重要性
- **Until Yongin**: M15X is sole capacity expansion avenue | 永亭之前：M15X是唯一产能扩张途径
- **HBM optimization**: Adjacent TSV equipment synergies | HBM优化：相邻TSV设备协同
- **Competition**: Critical for HBM market leadership vs Samsung | 竞争：对三星HBM市场领导地位至关重要
