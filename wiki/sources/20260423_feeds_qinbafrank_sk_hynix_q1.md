# SK Hynix Q1 2026 Blowout Earnings

## 📌 TL;DR
SK Hynix reported record-breaking Q1 2026 results: ₩52.58T revenue (+298% YoY), ₩37.61T operating profit (+505% YoY), 72% operating margin, driven by DRAM/NAND price increases and AI-driven HBM demand.
SK海力士公布了创纪录的2026年Q1业绩：营收525.8万亿韩元（同比+298%），营业利润376.1万亿韩元（同比+505%），营业利润率72%，由DRAM/NAND价格上涨和AI驱动的HBM需求驱动。

---

## 🗓️ Update Event
SK Hynix reported Q1 2026 earnings on April 23, 2026: ₩52.58T revenue (QoQ +160%, YoY +298%), ₩37.61T operating profit (QoQ +196%, YoY +505%), net profit ₩40.35T (77% net margin)—the highest single-quarter operating margin in company history.
SK海力士于2026年4月23日公布2026年Q1财报：营收525.8万亿韩元（环比+160%，同比+298%），营业利润376.1万亿韩元（环比+196%，同比+505%），净利润403.5万亿韩元（净利润率77%）——公司历史上最高的单季营业利润率。

## 🔎 Why It Matters
These results validate the AI memory supercycle thesis and confirm that HBM supply constraints are creating extraordinary pricing power for memory manufacturers. The 72% operating margin in a "commodity" semiconductor business is nearly unprecedented.
这些业绩验证了AI内存超级周期论，并确认HBM供应约束正在为内存制造商创造非凡的定价权。在"大宗商品"半导体业务中实现72%的营业利润率几乎是史无前例的。

## 🔍 Key Financial Breakdown

### Revenue Drivers
- **DRAM:** QoQ shipment volume (B/G) flat, but ASP +60% QoQ—a pure pricing story.
  DRAM：环比出货量持平，但平均售价环比上涨60%以上——纯粹的定价故事。
- **NAND:** QoQ shipment volume -10%, but ASP +70% QoQ—volume decline absorbed by pricing power.
  NAND：环比出货量下降10%，，但平均售价环比上涨70%以上——定价权吸收了销量下滑。

### AI Demand Tailwinds
The post argues that AI's evolution from training to inference and Agentic AI is expanding the total demand base for memory. AI agents generate and process significantly more data than passive inference, creating sustained demand pressure.
推文认为AI从训练向推理及Agentic AI的演进正在扩大内存总需求基础。AI代理比被动推理产生和处理更多数据，创造持续的需求压力。
