# Qinbafrank: March 2026 US CPI Data Analysis

## Summary | 摘要

Qinbafrank's analysis of March 2026 US CPI data, market outlook, and Iran war negotiations impact on "Spring Invasion" (春劫行情) market dynamics.

Qinbafrank对2026年3月美国CPI数据的分析，市场展望，以及伊朗战争谈判对"春劫行情"的影响。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### CPI Data | CPI数据
- March CPI: Headline met expectations, core CPI slightly below expectations YoY and MoM
- Data not as strong as feared
- Hope for April data continues

### Market Interpretation | 市场解读
- If CPI meets or beats expectations = inflation not as strong as feared
- Market naturally speculates if April data will also be good
- But Iran negotiations not fully resolved
- Ceasefire still not fully open

### "Spring Invasion" (春劫行情) | 春劫行情
- First real negotiation since Iran war outbreak
- War breakthrough = major market reversal
- But Iran conditions still harsh
- Several conditions Trump will have difficulty accepting
- Expect more volatility in negotiations

### Trading Strategy | 交易策略
- When negotiations get volatile again, market may panic
- That should NOT be the time for us to panic
- "Don't panic when market panics"
