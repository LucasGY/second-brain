# Paulo: Nittobo Strategic Shift - E-Glass Supply Gap

## Summary | 摘要

Nittobo is strategically exiting E-glass production, focusing on premium glass cloth. This creates a "structural gap" in E-glass supply, benefiting competitors.

日东纺正在战略性地退出E玻璃生产，专注于高级玻璃布。这在E玻璃供应中创造了"结构性缺口"，使竞争对手受益。

## Key Analysis | 关键分析

### E-Glass vs NE-Glass | E玻璃与NE玻璃
- **Price difference**: Up to 2x | 价格差高达2倍
- **E-glass**: Standard grade | 标准等级
- **NE-glass**: Premium for AI servers | AI服务器用高级品

### Market Impact | 市场影响
- Nittobo focusing on high-end | 日东纺专注高端
- Structural gap in E-glass | E玻璃结构性缺口
- Competitors benefit | 竞争对手受益
