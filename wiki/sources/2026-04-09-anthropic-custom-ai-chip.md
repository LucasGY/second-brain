# Anthropic 考虑开发自有AI芯片

Anthropic is reportedly considering developing its own AI chips, according to sources cited by Reuters.

Anthropic据报道正在考虑开发自有AI芯片，彭博社援引知情人士称。

- Anthropic weighs building its own AI chips
