# GS Global Strategy Views: The Technology Value Opportunity

## 📌 TL;DR
Goldman Sachs argues that global technology has de-rated faster than its earnings outlook has weakened, creating a “value within growth” opportunity rather than a classic bubble unwind.
Goldman Sachs 认为，全球科技板块的去估值速度快于盈利预期恶化速度，因此当前机会更像是“成长中的价值重估”，而不是典型泡沫破裂后的持续出清。

---

## 🎯 Core Investment Proposition
Can investors rotate back into global technology after its 2025 relative underperformance on the thesis that valuation has reset while earnings quality remains strong?
在 2025 年科技相对收益显著走弱之后，投资者是否可以基于“估值已重置、盈利质量仍强”的逻辑重新回到全球科技板块？

## 🔍 Critical Breakdown (Problem-Driven)
* **Question/Argument:** The 2025 drawdown in technology is primarily a valuation reset, not a full earnings-collapse regime.
  **问题/论点：** 2025 年科技板块的下跌主要是估值重置，而不是盈利全面塌陷。
  * **Their Evidence:** US equities underperformed other major regions; World Tech had one of its weakest relative periods versus World ex-TMT since the early 1970s; forward PEG for global IT fell below the global aggregate; software and hyperscaler valuation premia compressed sharply.
    **他们的证据：** 美股跑输其他主要地区；全球科技相对全球非 TMT 板块的表现达到自 1970 年代初以来最弱区间之一；全球 IT 的前瞻 PEG 低于全球整体市场；软件和 hyperscaler 的估值溢价显著压缩。
  * **Evidence Quality:** Strong on market-based relative measures and cross-checks between price, P/E, and PEG; weaker because consensus growth estimates can lag fast market repricing.
    **证据质量：** 在相对收益、P/E 和 PEG 的市场化指标上证据较强；但由于一致预期增速经常滞后于价格重定价，因此分母可能存在滞后偏差。
  * **Missing Variables:** Post-DeepSeek demand elasticity, the pace of future earnings downgrades, and whether higher real rates persist longer than expected.
    **缺失变量：** DeepSeek 之后需求弹性的真实变化、未来盈利下修的速度，以及更高实际利率是否会持续更久。
  * **Independent Assessment:** Persuasive as a tactical and selective re-entry thesis, but not yet enough to declare an all-clear for the entire technology complex.
    **独立判断：** 作为战术性、选择性回补科技的逻辑是有说服力的，但尚不足以宣布整个科技板块已经全面解除风险。

* **Question/Argument:** Hyperscaler capex fears are real, but the market may already be discounting too much balance-sheet and return-on-investment damage.
  **问题/论点：** Hyperscaler 资本开支压力真实存在，但市场可能已经过度计入了资产负债表和投资回报受损。
  * **Their Evidence:** Hyperscaler capex as a share of operating cash flow has risen rapidly; debt ratios for US tech increased but remain low; IT has the strongest EPS revisions; AI infrastructure spending still contributes materially to S&P 500 earnings growth.
    **他们的证据：** Hyperscaler 的资本开支占经营现金流比例快速上升；美国科技净负债率虽上升但仍偏低；IT 板块的 EPS 修正最强；AI 基础设施支出仍显著贡献标普 500 盈利增长。
  * **Evidence Quality:** Balanced and stronger than a pure bullish note because the report explicitly acknowledges capex, cash-flow, and terminal-value concerns.
    **证据质量：** 相比纯多头报告更均衡，因为其明确承认了资本开支、现金流和终值定价方面的担忧。
  * **Missing Variables:** Incremental ROI on AI capex, depreciation and energy costs, regulatory constraints, and whether spending can stay elevated if cloud demand softens.
    **缺失变量：** AI 增量资本开支的真实 ROI、折旧与能源成本、监管约束，以及若云需求放缓资本开支能否维持高位。
  * **Independent Assessment:** The better reading is not “capex risk is gone,” but “capex risk is now more fully priced than near-term earnings resilience.”
    **独立判断：** 更准确的解读不是“资本开支风险消失了”，而是“资本开支风险的定价已经比近期盈利韧性更充分”。

* **Question/Argument:** Today’s technology setup is not a classic bubble.
  **问题/论点：** 当前科技板块并不符合经典泡沫的特征。
  * **Their Evidence:** The Magnificent 7’s 24-month forward P/E and EV/Sales are far below the 2000 tech bubble leaders and below Japan’s late-1980s bubble extremes; equity issuance remains subdued versus the 1990s TMT boom.
    **他们的证据：** 当前 Mag 7 的 24 个月前瞻 P/E 和 EV/Sales 远低于 2000 年科技泡沫龙头，也低于 1980 年代末日本泡沫极值；股权融资发行规模相较 1990 年代 TMT 泡沫明显克制。
  * **Evidence Quality:** Useful rebuttal to simplistic bubble analogies because it combines valuation history with issuance behavior.
    **证据质量：** 因为同时使用历史估值和股权供给行为，这一证据对简单的“泡沫类比”具有较强反驳力。
  * **Missing Variables:** Index concentration, passive-flow reflexivity, private-market supply, and whether future mega-cap IPOs will absorb liquidity.
    **缺失变量：** 指数集中度、被动资金的反身性、一级市场供给，以及未来大型科技 IPO 是否会吸收流动性。
  * **Independent Assessment:** Strong as a valuation-history argument, but it supports selective accumulation rather than a blanket sector overweight.
    **独立判断：** 作为历史估值比较，它相当有力；但更支持“精选增配”，而不是“全板块无差别加仓”。

## 👻 Implicit Assumptions
- Consensus FY2 EPS growth for technology will not be materially revised down in the next few quarters.
  科技板块的 FY2 一致预期 EPS 增速在未来几个季度不会被大幅下修。
- AI infrastructure spending will continue despite macro and geopolitical uncertainty.
  尽管存在宏观与地缘政治不确定性，AI 基础设施支出仍会持续。
- Rising capex lowers future returns less than current market prices imply.
  资本开支抬升对未来回报率的压制，小于当前市场价格所隐含的悲观程度。
- The Iran / Strait of Hormuz shock will eventually cap rates through growth fears rather than trigger a durable inflation spiral.
  伊朗 / 霍尔木兹海峡冲击最终更可能通过增长担忧压制利率，而不是引发持久性的通胀螺旋。
- Old-economy “HALO effect” beneficiaries will not permanently absorb all capital away from software and hyperscalers.
  受益于“HALO effect”的旧经济板块不会长期吸走原本属于软件和 hyperscaler 的全部配置资金。

## ⚡ Alpha & Expectation Gap
* **Market Consensus (Priced In):** Technology is being punished for capex intensity, AI disruption risk, and long-duration rate sensitivity; software multiples have already compressed sharply.
  **市场共识（已计价）：** 市场已经围绕资本开支强度、AI 颠覆风险和长久期利率敏感性惩罚科技板块；软件估值倍数已经明显压缩。
* **Marginal Information:** The report’s real edge is the combination of valuation reset and still-strong earnings revisions, especially the unusually wide gap between price underperformance and earnings resilience.
  **边际信息：** 这篇报告真正的增量在于把“估值重置”和“盈利修正仍强”组合起来，尤其强调了股价跑输与盈利韧性之间异常扩大的背离。

## 📈 Key Tracking Metrics
1. Relative return of World IT versus World ex-TMT, plus software’s forward P/E premium versus the market.
   全球 IT 相对全球非 TMT 的相对收益，以及软件板块相对市场的前瞻 P/E 溢价。
2. Forward PEG for MSCI AC World IT versus MSCI AC World.
   MSCI AC World IT 相对 MSCI AC World 的前瞻 PEG。
3. Hyperscaler CAPEX / CFO and net debt / equity.
   Hyperscaler 的 CAPEX / CFO 和净负债 / 权益比。
4. IT EPS revisions and FY2 EPS growth versus price performance.
   IT 板块 EPS 修正、FY2 EPS 增长与股价表现之间的背离。
5. Share of S&P 500 EPS growth attributable to AI infrastructure and cloud services.
   AI 基础设施和云服务对标普 500 EPS 增长的贡献占比。
6. Rolling 12-month count of IPOs and secondary issuance in US TMT.
   美国 TMT 板块滚动 12 个月 IPO 与增发数量。
