# Central Bank Gold Reserves Surpass Dollar Holdings

## Summary | 摘要

For first time since IMF data collection (late 1990s), dollar-denominated central bank reserves adjusted for valuation now lower than gold reserves.

自IMF收集数据以来（1990年代末），经估值调整的央行美元储备首次低于黄金储备。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Dollar Reserves | 美元储备
- Central bank holdings
- Adjusted for valuation effects
- Now below gold reserves

### Historical Significance | 历史意义
- First time since late 1990s
- Indicates de-dollarization trend
- Central banks diversifying
