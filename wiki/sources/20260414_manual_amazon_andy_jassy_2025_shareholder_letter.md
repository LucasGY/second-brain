# Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's 2025 Letter to Shareholders

## 📌 TL;DR
Amazon frames 2025 as proof that durable company-building requires non-linear execution: inventing new inflections, running multiple strategic paths in parallel, and investing aggressively in AI infrastructure before revenue fully arrives.
亚马逊将 2025 年定义为一个例证：真正可持续的公司建设并不是线性推进，而是要主动发明新拐点、并行推进多条战略路径，并在收入完全兑现前就重投 AI 基础设施。

## 🎯 Core Investment Proposition
The letter argues that Amazon's medium- and long-term upside depends on making large, sometimes uncomfortable investments before the economics look tidy, especially in AI infrastructure, robotics, logistics, and next-generation interfaces.
这封信的核心论点是：亚马逊的中长期上行空间，取决于它是否愿意在财务表面尚不“漂亮”时就提前进行大额投入，尤其是在 AI 基础设施、机器人、物流和下一代交互界面上。

The implied shareholder ask is patience: management wants investors to tolerate weaker near-term free cash flow in exchange for future revenue scale, operating leverage, and a stronger strategic position.
它对股东的隐含要求是耐心：管理层希望投资者接受短期自由现金流承压，以换取未来更大的收入规模、更强的经营杠杆和更稳固的战略位置。

## 🔍 Critical Breakdown (Problem-Driven)
* **Question/Argument:** Amazon should invest ahead of realized AI demand because this capex wave can become a major long-term cash-flow engine.
  * **问题 / 论点：** 亚马逊应该在 AI 需求完全兑现之前就提前投资，因为这一轮资本开支有机会演化为长期的重要现金流引擎。
  * **Their Evidence:** Jassy says AWS demand materially exceeds current supply, large customer commitments already exist, and capex-funded assets such as datacenters and servers monetize over multi-year windows.
  * **对方给出的证据：** Jassy 表示 AWS 需求明显超过当前供给，已有大型客户承诺，而且数据中心、服务器等由资本开支支持的资产会在多年周期内逐步变现。
  * **Evidence Quality:** The logic is coherent and consistent with hyperscaler economics, but the public letter offers directional claims rather than contract-level detail, utilization curves, or return thresholds.
  * **证据质量：** 这套逻辑是自洽的，也符合超大规模云厂商的经济规律，但公开信提供的更多是方向性陈述，而不是合同级细节、利用率曲线或回报门槛。
  * **Missing Variables:** Competitive pricing pressure, model efficiency gains that reduce compute demand, regulatory friction, and execution risk in custom silicon are not explored in depth.
  * **缺失变量：** 竞争定价压力、模型效率提升导致算力需求下降、监管摩擦，以及自研芯片执行风险，都没有被充分展开。
  * **Independent Assessment:** The thesis is plausible because supply-constrained infrastructure businesses often need pre-build investment, but the investment case rests on Amazon sustaining both demand growth and healthy post-deployment utilization.
  * **独立判断：** 这个论点是成立的，因为供给受限的基础设施业务通常确实需要先建后卖；但投资逻辑最终仍取决于亚马逊能否同时维持需求增长和部署后的健康利用率。
* **Question/Argument:** Parallel strategic bets outperform single-track execution when the future state is uncertain.
  * **问题 / 论点：** 当未来状态高度不确定时，并行战略下注优于单线推进。
  * **Their Evidence:** Amazon cites same-day fulfillment centers, Prime Air, and Amazon Now as complementary paths for faster delivery rather than mutually exclusive choices.
  * **对方给出的证据：** 亚马逊把 Same Day Fulfillment Centers、Prime Air 和 Amazon Now 视为加速配送的互补路径，而不是彼此排斥的单选项。
  * **Evidence Quality:** The examples are concrete and operationally grounded, showing how parallel bets can share infrastructure and attack different customer jobs-to-be-done.
  * **证据质量：** 这些例子足够具体，也有运营层面的落点，说明并行下注如何共享基础设施，并覆盖不同的客户任务场景。
  * **Missing Variables:** The letter does not quantify failure rates, organizational overhead, or the cost of supporting too many simultaneous experiments.
  * **缺失变量：** 这封信没有量化失败率、组织管理开销，或同时支持过多实验的成本。
  * **Independent Assessment:** This is a strong management principle when paired with disciplined portfolio review; without that discipline, "2 > 0" can degrade into fragmented resource allocation.
  * **独立判断：** 如果能配合严格的组合管理与复盘机制，这是一条很强的管理原则；如果没有这种纪律，`2 > 0` 也可能退化为资源分散和注意力碎片化。
* **Question/Argument:** Mature products sometimes need to go back to first principles rather than layering incremental upgrades.
  * **问题 / 论点：** 成熟产品有时需要回到第一性原理重新设计，而不是持续叠加渐进式补丁。
  * **Their Evidence:** Bedrock reportedly rebuilt its inference engine into Mantle in 76 days using Kiro-style agentic coding support, and Alexa+ required a full architectural rethink.
  * **对方给出的证据：** Bedrock 据称借助类似 Kiro 的 agentic coding 支持，在 76 天内把推理引擎重构为 Mantle；而 Alexa+ 也需要一次完整的架构重想。
  * **Evidence Quality:** These examples are vivid and strategically meaningful, though they are management-selected success stories and do not show the costs or failed resets behind the scenes.
  * **证据质量：** 这些案例很鲜活，也具有明确战略意义，但它们毕竟是管理层挑选出来的成功故事，没有展示背后的成本和失败重构案例。
  * **Missing Variables:** Migration risk, service reliability tradeoffs, and how broadly these resets can be repeated across the company are left unstated.
  * **缺失变量：** 迁移风险、服务可靠性的取舍，以及这种“推倒重来”能否在全公司范围内复制，这些都没有被明确说明。
  * **Independent Assessment:** The lesson is durable: AI-era disruption increases the value of architectural resets, but only teams with strong operational discipline can execute them without damaging the installed base.
  * **独立判断：** 这个结论具有长期价值：AI 时代的冲击确实提高了架构级重构的价值，但只有运营纪律很强的团队，才能在不伤害现有用户基础的前提下完成这种重构。

## 👻 Implicit Assumptions
Amazon assumes AI demand will remain structurally supply-constrained long enough for pre-built capacity to earn attractive returns.
亚马逊默认 AI 需求会在足够长的时间内维持结构性供给紧张，从而让提前建设的产能获得可观回报。

Amazon assumes its breadth across AWS, logistics, consumer surfaces, and devices creates cross-business compounding rather than coordination drag.
亚马逊默认自己横跨 AWS、物流、消费端入口和设备的广度，会形成跨业务复利，而不是协同拖累。

Management assumes shareholders will reward long-duration strategic positioning even when reported free cash flow temporarily weakens.
管理层默认，即使报告口径下的自由现金流短期走弱，股东仍会为这种长期战略卡位买单。

The letter assumes Amazon can keep organizational speed high while simultaneously flattening structure and increasing the number of frontier bets.
这封信默认，亚马逊可以在组织扁平化的同时继续保持高速度，并并行推进更多前沿下注。

## ⚡ Alpha & Expectation Gap (预期差)
* **Market Consensus (Priced In):** Investors already know Amazon is spending heavily on AI infrastructure and that AWS is a central growth engine.
  * **市场共识（已被计价）:** 投资者已经知道亚马逊正在大举投入 AI 基础设施，也知道 AWS 是核心增长引擎。
* **Marginal Information:** The letter adds management's explicit willingness to over-invest through free-cash-flow pressure, its claim that large customer commitments de-risk a meaningful portion of the spend, and its broader view that AI should trigger product-level reinvention across the company.
  * **边际信息:** 这封信新增了三个更明确的信号：管理层愿意在自由现金流承压时继续超前投资；大量客户承诺已在一定程度上为支出去风险；以及 AI 将推动公司级别的产品重构，而不是仅仅作为局部功能增强。

## 📈 Key Tracking Metrics
1. AWS revenue growth versus capex growth and the lagged recovery in free cash flow.
   AWS 收入增速相对于资本开支增速，以及随后自由现金流的修复节奏。
2. Evidence that new bets such as robotics, Prime Air, Amazon Now, and Alexa+ convert engagement gains into durable economics.
   机器人、Prime Air、Amazon Now 和 Alexa+ 等新下注，是否能把使用提升转化为可持续经济回报。
