# Superwhisper新语音模型：太强无法发布

## Summary | 摘要

Superwhisper's new voice model so powerful it started finishing sentences users hadn't thought of yet. Identified a flaw in English language that went unnoticed for 600 years. Withheld from public release.

Superwhisper的新语音模型强大到能完成用户还没想到的句子。发现了一个英语中存在600年未被注意到的缺陷。暂不公开发布。
