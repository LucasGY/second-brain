# SK海力士：存储器热潮，利润超越微软和谷歌

## Summary | 摘要

According to KB Securities research, SK Hynix is projected to surpass Microsoft and Google in operating profit in 2026, with 2027 operating profit expected to reach 358 trillion won.

根据KB证券研究报告，SK海力士预计在2026年超越微软和谷歌的营业利润，2027年营业利润预计达到358万亿韩元。

## Key Points | 关键信息

- KB Securities raises target price to 1.9 million KRW
- SK Hynix projected to enter world's top 3 operating profit
- 2027 operating profit forecast: 358 trillion won
- Unprecedented memory boom driven by AI
