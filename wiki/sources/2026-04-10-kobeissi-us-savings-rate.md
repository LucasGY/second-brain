# Kobeissi Letter: US Personal Savings Rate Drops to 4.0%

## Summary | 摘要

US personal savings rate dropped to 4.0% in February 2026, the 2nd-lowest since November 2022. This is the lowest level since the 2008 Financial Crisis (excluding 2022).

美国个人储蓄率在2026年2月降至4.0%，为2022年11月以来的第二低。除2022年外，这是2008年金融危机以来的最低水平。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Savings Rate | 储蓄率
- **February 2026**: 4.0% | 4.0%
- **Change**: -0.5 percentage points | 下降0.5个百分点
- **Lowest since**: November 2022 | 2022年11月以来最低
- **Historical context**: Lowest since 2008 Financial Crisis | 2008年金融危机以来最低

### Comparison | 对比
- **5-year average**: 6.5% | 五年平均6.5%
- **5-year pre-pandemic average**: 6.0% | 疫情前五年平均6.0%
- **Decline since April 2025**: -1.5 percentage points | 2025年4月以来下降1.5个百分点

### Factors | 因素
- Real disposable income growth has weakened | 实际可支配收入增长疲弱
- Households limiting ability to rebuild savings | 家庭重建储蓄能力受限
- Inflation eroding purchasing power | 通胀侵蚀购买力

## Implications | 影响
- Consumer spending may be constrained | 消费支出可能受限
- Economic growth outlook pressured | 经济增长前景承压
- Fed rate cut expectations affected | 美联储降息预期受影响
