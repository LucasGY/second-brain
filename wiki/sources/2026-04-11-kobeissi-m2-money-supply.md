# Kobeissi Letter: US M2 Money Supply at Record $22.6T

## Summary | 摘要

US M2 money supply jumped +4.8% YoY in February 2026 to a record $22.6 trillion, signaling potential inflation pressures ahead.

美国M2货币供应量在2026年2月同比增长4.8%，达到创纪录的22.6万亿美元，预示未来潜在通胀压力。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### M2 Money Supply | M2货币供应
- **February 2026**: $22.6 trillion | 22.6万亿美元
- **YoY Growth**: +4.8% | 同比增长4.8%
- **Record high**: All-time high | 创历史新高

### Inflation Implications | 通胀影响
- Money supply growth accelerating | 货币供应增长加速
- Potential inflation ahead | 潜在通胀前景
