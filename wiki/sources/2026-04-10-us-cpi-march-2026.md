# 美国2026年3月CPI数据发布

## Summary | 摘要

US March 2026 CPI rose to 3.3%, below expectations of 3.4%. Core CPI rose to 2.6%, below expectations of 2.7%. However, CPI is now at its highest level since May 2024 amid the Iran War. Fed rate cuts have been priced out for 2026.

美国2026年3月CPI上涨至3.3%，低于预期的3.4%。核心CPI上涨至2.6%，低于预期的2.7%。然而，受伊朗战争影响，CPI现已达到2024年5月以来最高水平。2026年美联储降息已被市场消化。

## Key Points | 关键信息

- Overall CPI: 3.3% (vs 3.4% expected)
- Core CPI: 2.6% (vs 2.7% expected)
- Highest CPI since May 2024
- Fed rate cuts priced out for 2026
