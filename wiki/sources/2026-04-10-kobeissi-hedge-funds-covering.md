# Kobeissi Letter: Hedge Funds Unwinding Shorts

## Summary | 摘要

Hedge funds are unwinding their short exposure. Short positions in US macro products dropped to 11.2%, the lowest since early March. Largest single-day short covering in 10 years.

对冲基金正在平掉空头头寸。美国宏观产品空头头寸降至11.2%，为3月初以来最低。10年来最大的单日空头回补。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Short Covering | 空头回补
- **Current short exposure**: 11.2% | 当前空头敞口11.2%
- **2-day change**: -1.0 percentage points | 两天下降1个百分点
- **ETF shorts**: -7.4% in one day | 单日下降7.4%

### Historical Context | 历史背景
- Largest 1-day drop since August 2020 | 2020年8月以来最大单日降幅
- Largest short covering in 10 years | 10年来最大空头回补
- Still 4% above 2024 lows | 仍比2024年低点高4%

## Market Implications | 市场影响
- Short covering driving rally | 空头回补推动反弹
- Hedge funds still have exposure | 对冲基金仍有空头敞口
- Momentum likely to continue | 动能可能持续
