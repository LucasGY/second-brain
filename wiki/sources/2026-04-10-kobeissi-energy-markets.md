# Kobeissi Letter: Energy & Economic Update

## Summary | 摘要

The Kobeissi Letter reports on multiple breaking economic indicators for April 10, 2026: crude oil exports surge, gas price inflation, savings rate decline, and hedge fund positioning.

Kobeissi Letter报道2026年4月10日多个重要经济指标：原油出口激增、汽油通胀、储蓄率下降、对冲基金仓位。

## Key Reports | 主要报道

### US Crude Oil Exports Surge | 美国原油出口激增
- April exports: 4.90 million barrels/day (record)
- +23% since March, +30% since February
- May expected to exceed 5.0 million barrels/day first time ever
- 28 supertankers booked vs typical 5
- Asian buyers securing US cargoes amid Middle East supply loss

### Gas Price Inflation | 汽油价格通胀
- Largest monthly CPI jump since 1967
- Related to Iran War geopolitical tensions

### US Personal Savings Rate | 个人储蓄率
- Dropped to 4.0% in February
- 2nd lowest since November 2022
- -1.5 percentage points since April 2025
- Pre-pandemic 5-year average: 6.0%
- Last 5-year average: 6.5%

### Hedge Fund Positioning | 对冲基金仓位
- Short positions down -1.0% over 2 days to 11.2%
- ETF shorts fell -7.4% Wednesday (largest since August 2020)
- Largest single-day short covering in 10 years
- Still 4 percentage points above 2024 low

### Iran Geopolitics | 伊朗地缘政治
- Trump: Iran "has no cards" other than Strait of Hormuz
