# Kobeissi Letter: US Crude Exports at Record 4.9M Barrels/Day

## Summary | 摘要

US Gulf Coast crude exports on track to average record 4.90 million barrels/day in April. Up +30% since February. 28 supertankers lined up for May.

美国墨西哥湾沿岸原油出口4月份平均有望达到创纪录的每日490万桶。自2月以来增长30%。5月份已安排28艘超级油轮。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Export Surge | 出口激增
- **April average**: 4.90 million barrels/day | 每日490万桶
- **March comparison**: +930,000 b/d increase | 增长93万桶/日
- **February comparison**: +1.12 million b/d increase | 增长112万桶/日

### Future Outlook | 未来展望
- **May projection**: 5.0+ million barrels/day | 超过500万桶/日
- **Supertankers booked**: 28 for May | 5月份已订28艘
- **Typical May bookings**: 5 at this point | 通常此时订5艘

### Drivers | 驱动因素
- Asian buyers offsetting Middle East losses | 亚洲买家弥补中东损失
- Strait of Hormuz limited flows | 霍尔木兹海峡流量受限
- Global demand for American oil highest ever | 全球对美国石油需求创历史新高
