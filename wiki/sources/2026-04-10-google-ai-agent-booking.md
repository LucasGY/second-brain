# Google AI Mode: Agentic Restaurant Booking

## Summary | 摘要

Google expands AI Mode agentic capabilities to restaurant booking. Users can describe preferences and AI books across multiple platforms. Expanding to 8 new countries.

Google将AI Mode的智能体能力扩展到餐厅预订。用户可以描述偏好，AI跨平台预订。扩展至8个新国家。

## Key Features | 主要功能
- Describe preferences in natural language
- AI searches multiple platforms
- Real-time availability check
- One-tap booking via partners

## Expansion | 扩展
- Originally US only
- Now: Australia, Canada, Hong Kong, India, NZ, Singapore, South Africa, UK
