# Thariq: Claude Code TurboTax Connector

## Summary | 摘要

Claude Code now has TurboTax connector integration. Users can connect TurboTax or Aiwyn Tax to Claude to estimate refunds, see what they owe, and understand tax forms.

Claude Code现在有TurboTax连接器集成。用户可以将TurboTax或Aiwyn Tax连接到Claude来估算退税、查看欠款和了解税务表格。

## Key Features | 关键功能

### Tax Integration | 税务集成
- **TurboTax connector**: Available now | 现在可用
- **Aiwyn Tax**: Formerly Column Tax | 原Column Tax
- **Features**: Estimate refund, see owed amount, understand forms | 功能：估算退税、查看欠款、了解表格

### Use Cases | 用例
- Tax estimation with AI | 使用AI进行税务估算
- Form understanding | 表格理解
- procrastination-friendly | 适合拖延者
