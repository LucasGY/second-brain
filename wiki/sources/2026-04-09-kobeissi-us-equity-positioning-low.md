# Kobeissi: US Equity Positioning at Historically Low Levels

## Summary | 摘要

US equity positioning has fallen to historically low levels. The US consolidated equity positioning is down to its lowest since May 2025, posting a similar decline as during the first months of the 2022 bear market.

美国股票持仓已降至历史低位。美国综合股票持仓降至2025年5月以来最低，跌幅与2022年熊市初期类似。

## Key Points | 关键要点

### Historic Lows | 历史低位
- **Current**: Lowest since May 2025 | 当前：2025年5月以来最低
- **Similar decline**: As during early 2022 bear market | 跌幅：与2022年熊市初期类似
- **Coverage**: Professional investors, fund managers, hedge funds, algos | 范围：专业投资者、基金经理、对冲基金、量化基金

### Positioning Levels | 持仓水平
- **Over 15 years**: Only lower 20% of the time | 15年来：仅20%的时间更低
- **Discretionary investors**: Underweight 16% of the time | 自主投资者：仅16%的时间低配
- **Systematic funds (CTAs)**: Levels only seen 23% of the time | 系统性基金（CTA）：仅23%的时间达到此水平

### Market Implications | 市场影响

#### Contrarian Opportunity | 反向机会
- Positioning so low that any positive catalyst could fuel sharp rebound | 持仓如此之低，任何积极催化剂都可能引发强劲反弹
- Classic contrarian indicator | 经典反向指标
- Risk/reward increasingly favorable | 风险/回报越来越有利
