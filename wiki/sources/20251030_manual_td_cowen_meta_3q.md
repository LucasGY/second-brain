# TD Cowen 3Q Review: Strong Results Overshadowed by AI Infrastructure

## 📌 TL;DR
TD Cowen views Meta's 3Q results as solid but warns that aggressive AI infrastructure spending will weigh on profitability in coming quarters, creating a "show me" dynamic for AI ROI.
TD Cowen 认为 Meta 的 3Q 业绩稳健，但警告激进的 AI 基础设施投入将在未来几个季度拖累盈利能力，形成"向我证明"的 AI ROI 动态。

---

## 🎯 Core Investment Proposition
强劲的 3Q 业绩能否平息投资者对 AI 基础设施支出和利润率侵蚀的担忧？
Can strong 3Q results quell investor concerns about AI infrastructure spending and margin erosion?

## 🔍 Critical Breakdown

* **Question/Argument:** Core business fundamentals remain strong
  * **Their Evidence:** Revenue growth driven by ad volume and pricing; Family DAU growth positive
  * **Evidence Quality:** Solid quarter supports near-term bull case
  * **Missing Variables:** macro sensitivity of SMB advertisers
  * **Independent Assessment:** Business is healthy; execution concerns are medium-term

* **Question/Argument:** AI infrastructure ramp creates visibility challenge
  * **Their Evidence:** Capex guidance raised; management signals multi-year investment horizon
  * **Evidence Quality:** Credible given competitive dynamics, but earnings uncertainty elevated
  * **Missing Variables:** Specific ROI timeline or milestones
  * **Independent Assessment:** The "show me" phase for AI monetization has begun

* **Question/Argument:** Reels and new products provide optionality
  * **Their Evidence:** Short-form video monetization improving; Threads engagement trending up
  * **Evidence Quality:** Early stage but directionally positive
  * **Missing Variables:** Monetization timeline and margin profile
  * **Independent Assessment:** Nice optionality but not material near-term

## 👻 Implicit Assumptions

* AI infrastructure spending will not face diminishing returns
* Management's AI monetization timeline is achievable
* Competition will not close the AI ad tech gap rapidly

## ⚡ Alpha & Expectation Gap

* **Market Consensus (Priced In):** Results expected; focus shifts to AI spending trajectory
* **Marginal Information:** "Show me" framing captures investor sentiment shift

## 📈 Key Tracking Metrics

1. Quarterly Capex vs. Prior Guidance
2. Revenue per Family DAU
3. Reels Revenue Contribution
4. Threads Monetization Milestones
