# Kobeissi Letter: Healthcare Carrying US Job Growth

## Summary | 摘要

Healthcare is carrying US job growth more than ever. The number of healthcare workers has surged 84% since 1996, far outpacing other sectors.

医疗保健比以往任何时候都更多地支撑着美国就业增长。自1996年以来，医疗保健工作者数量激增84%，远超其他行业。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Healthcare Employment | 医疗保健就业
- **Growth since 1996**: +84% | 增长84%
- **vs Other sectors**: Far outpacing | 远超其他行业
- **Trend**: Accelerating | 持续加速

### Economic Implications | 经济影响
- Healthcare becoming dominant employment sector | 医疗保健成为主导就业部门
- Wage growth in healthcare | 医疗保健工资增长
- Cost pressures on economy | 经济成本压力
