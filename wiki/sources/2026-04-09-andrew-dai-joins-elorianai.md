# Jeff Dean RT: Andrew Dai Leaves DeepMind After 12 Years for ElorianAI

## Summary | 摘要

Andrew M. Dai announces departure from Google Brain/DeepMind after almost 12 years to co-found ElorianAI, a new multimodal reasoning lab with former LLM pretraining, data, and multimodal leads.

Andrew M. Dai宣布离开Google Brain/DeepMind，在近12年后与前LLM预训练、数据和多模态负责人共同创立ElorianAI——一个新的多模态推理实验室。
