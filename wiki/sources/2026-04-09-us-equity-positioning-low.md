# US Equity Positioning at Historical Lows

## Summary | 摘要

US equity positioning has fallen to lowest since May 2025. Hedge funds and systematic funds at historically low levels, creating potential for sharp rebound.

美国股票仓位降至2025年5月以来最低。对冲基金和系统化基金仓位处于历史低位，为强劲反弹创造条件。

## Key Data | 关键数据

### Positioning Levels | 仓位水平
- Lowest since May 2025
- Similar to early 2022 bear market
- Lower only 20% of time in 15 years

### Investor Categories | 投资者类别
- Discretionary (hedge/mutual funds): Underweight at 16% of time
- Systematic (CTAs/vol-targeting): Levels only seen 23% of time

### Implication | 影响
- Any positive catalyst could fuel sharp rebound
- Positioning extremely bearish
- Contrarian opportunity
