# Andrew Dai Leaves DeepMind to Found ElorianAI

## Summary | 摘要

Andrew Dai, after nearly 12 years at Google Brain/DeepMind, has left to co-found ElorianAI - a new multimodal reasoning lab. The company is founded and led by former LLM pretraining, data, and multimodal leads.

Andrew Dai在谷歌大脑/DeepMind工作近12年后离职，联合创立了ElorianAI——一家新的多模态推理实验室。公司由前LLM预训练、数据和多模态负责人创立和领导。

## Key Details | 关键细节

### Founder Background | 创始人背景
- Andrew Dai: Nearly 12 years at Google Brain/DeepMind
- Co-founders: @yinfeiy, Seth
- Founded: @ElorianAI
- Focus: First multimodal reasoning lab

### Company Vision | 公司愿景
- Multimodal reasoning
- Led by former LLM pretraining leads
- Led by former data leads
- Led by former multimodal leads
