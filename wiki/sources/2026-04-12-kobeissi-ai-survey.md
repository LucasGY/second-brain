# Kobeissi Letter: Americans Expect Unemployment to Rise

## Summary | 摘要

Americans increasingly expect unemployment to rise. The perceived probability among US consumers that the unemployment rate will increase has reached concerning levels.

美国人越来越预期失业率上升。美国消费者认为失业率将上升的预期概率已达到令人担忧的水平。
