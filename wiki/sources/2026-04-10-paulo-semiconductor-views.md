# Paulo: Additional Semiconductor Views

## Summary | 摘要

Additional semiconductor analysis from Paulo covering memory boom, DRAM supply constraints, and AI chip competition dynamics.

Paulo补充半导体分析，涵盖内存热潮、DRAM供应限制和AI芯片竞争动态。

## Key Topics | 主要话题

### Memory Market | 内存市场
- Memory experiencing unprecedented boom
- SK Hynix projected to surpass Microsoft and Google operating profit in 2026
- KB Securities raises target to 1,900,000 KRW
- SK Hynix 2027 operating profit projected at 358T won (3rd globally)

### DRAM Supply Constraints | DRAM供应限制
- Only Samsung and CXMT can scale production significantly
- Both companies are busy with HBM production
- AI data centers consuming all available DRAM supply
- DRAM prices unlikely to fall while AI demand continues

### AI Chip Competition | AI芯片竞争
- GPU = NVIDIA dominance
- AI ASIC = Broadcom as primary competitor
- "Companies not growing faster than TSMC shouldn't be called growth companies"

### Hidden AI Plays | 隐藏AI标的
- Fuji箔 (Fuji Precision) - AI stock surge, year-to-date highs
- Soft pads for wafer polishing (like cat paw pads)
- Seiko Giken (精工技研) - daily outperformer
- Nittobo (日東紡) - substrate materials
