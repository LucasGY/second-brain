# 3月美国CPI分析：低于预期，市场松了一口气

## Summary | 摘要

US March CPI data came in below expectations - headline and core both slightly lower than forecast. This suggests March inflation wasn't as strong as feared, bringing hope for April data. However, geopolitical tensions (Strait) remain a concern.

美国3月CPI数据低于预期——总体和核心CPI都略低于预期。这表明3月通胀没有想象中那么强，给4月数据带来希望。然而，地缘政治紧张（台海）仍是隐忧。

## Key Points | 关键信息

- Headline CPI met expectations, core slightly below
- March inflation not as strong as feared
- Hope for April data
- Weekend negotiations and Iran developments being watched
