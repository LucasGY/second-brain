# Paulo: Bear Market Corrections and AI Revolution

## Summary | 摘要

Paulo critiques bear market predictors and short sellers. Argues AI revolution is inevitable, computing resources are fundamentally insufficient, and AI services should be used for market analysis.

Paulo批评熊市预测者和做空者。认为AI革命不可避免，计算资源根本不足，应该使用AI服务进行市场分析。

## Key Insights | 关键洞见

### Market Corrections | 市场调整
- **Bear predictions**: Habit-forming, not accurate | 熊市预测是习惯性的，不准确
- **Short selling**: Easy to get wrong | 做空容易出错
- **Correction**: Just adjust and move on | 修正后继续前进

### AI Revolution | AI革命
- **Inevitable**: Using AI services makes this clear | 使用AI服务能清楚看到这一点
- **Insufficient resources**: Computing power lacking | 计算资源不足
- **Price increases**: Willing to pay more | 愿意支付更多

### Paulo's View | Paulo观点
> "Computing resources are fundamentally insufficient"
> 计算资源根本不足
