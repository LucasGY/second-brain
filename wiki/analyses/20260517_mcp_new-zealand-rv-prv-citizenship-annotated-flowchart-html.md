## Display model

> ## 展示模型

The HTML page should have these sections:

> HTML 页面应包含以下区域：

1. Hero / TL;DR area
   - Title: New Zealand RV to PRV to Citizenship: Clickable Decision Flow.
   - Four prominent numbers: 2y, 184, 240, 1350.
   - Short statement: PRV does not normally reset the citizenship clock.

> 1. Hero / TL;DR 区
>    - 标题：新西兰 RV 到 PRV 再到入籍：点击式判断流程。
>    - 四个突出数字：2y、184、240、1350。
>    - 简短结论：PRV 通常不会重置入籍 5 年计时。

2. Main annotated flowchart
   - Left/main area: clickable flow nodes.
   - Right side: detail panel that changes when a node is clicked.
   - Legend: process, decision, pass path, fail path, terminal.

> 2. 主 annotated flowchart
>    - 左侧 / 中间：可点击流程节点。
>    - 右侧：详情面板，点击不同节点后动态切换。
>    - 图例：process、decision、pass path、fail path、terminal。

3. Flow nodes
   - Start: Resident Visa / resident status begins.
   - Decision: Has resident status reached 2 years?
   - Fail path: wait until the 2-year anniversary.
   - Decision: Do the two 12-month blocks before PRV application each have 184+ resident days in New Zealand?
   - Fail path: delay PRV or consider another commitment route.
   - Pass path: possible PRV application point.
   - Process: continue accumulating resident days for citizenship.
   - Decision: in the past 5 years before citizenship application, does each 12-month block have 240+ days and does the total reach 1,350+ days?
   - Fail path: continue accumulating presence.
   - Terminal: possible citizenship by grant application point.

> 3. 流程节点
>    - 起点：拿到 Resident Visa / 开始 resident 身份。
>    - 判断：resident 身份是否已经满 2 年？
>    - 否：继续等待 2 年周年日。
>    - 判断：PRV 申请日前两个 12 个月区间是否各有 184+ resident 天在新西兰？
>    - 否：推迟 PRV 或研究其他 commitment 路径。
>    - 是：可能达到 PRV 申请节点。
>    - 继续：累计入籍用 resident days。
>    - 判断：国籍申请日前过去 5 年，是否每个 12 个月 240+ 天，且总计 1,350+ 天？
>    - 否：继续累计 presence。
>    - 终点：可能达到 citizenship by grant 申请节点。

4. Rule cards
   - PRV 2-year threshold.
   - PRV 184/184 residence commitment rule.
   - Citizenship 240 x 5 plus 1,350 rule.

> 4. 规则卡片
>    - PRV 的 2 年门槛。
>    - PRV 的 184/184 居住 commitment 规则。
>    - 入籍的 240 x 5 + 1,350 规则。

5. Example timeline
   - 2024-07-01: RV granted in New Zealand.
   - 2024-07-01 to 2025-06-30: first PRV 12-month block, target 184+ days.
   - 2025-07-01 to 2026-06-30: second PRV 12-month block, target 184+ days.
   - 2026-07-01: possible earliest PRV point if all conditions are met.
   - 2024-07-01 to 2029-06-30: citizenship 5-year window.
   - Around 2029-07-01: possible citizenship application point if all conditions are met.

> 5. 示例时间线
>    - 2024-07-01：人在新西兰境内获批 RV。
>    - 2024-07-01 到 2025-06-30：PRV 第一个 12 个月区间，目标 184+ 天。
>    - 2025-07-01 到 2026-06-30：PRV 第二个 12 个月区间，目标 184+ 天。
>    - 2026-07-01：如果条件满足，可能是最早 PRV 节点。
>    - 2024-07-01 到 2029-06-30：入籍 5 年窗口。
>    - 约 2029-07-01：如果条件满足，可能是最早入籍申请节点。

6. Backward-counting table
   - PRV: count backward 24 months from actual PRV application date.
   - Citizenship: count backward 5 years from actual citizenship application date.

> 6. 倒推检查表
>    - PRV：从实际 PRV 申请日往前倒推 24 个月。
>    - 入籍：从实际国籍申请日往前倒推 5 年。

7. FAQ / traps
   - Second-year day 184 alone is not enough for PRV if the 2-year resident-status anniversary has not arrived.
   - Citizenship normally does not start only from PRV; RV/resident time can usually count.
   - If 184/184 is not met, other PRV commitment paths may exist but require specific evidence.
   - PRV still matters because of travel conditions.

> 7. FAQ / 陷阱
>    - 第二年住满第 184 天，不代表 resident 未满 2 年时就能申请 PRV。
>    - 入籍通常不是只从 PRV 开始算；RV/resident 时间通常可以算。
>    - 如果不满足 184/184，可能有其他 PRV commitment 路径，但更依赖具体证据。
>    - PRV 仍然重要，因为它通常解决 travel conditions 和离境返回稳定性问题。

## Right-side detail panel content

> ## 右侧详情面板内容

Each clicked node should show:

> 每个点击节点应显示：

- Node type.
- Short rule summary.
- Evidence or records to track.
- Risk / common mistake.
- Next action.

> - 节点类型。
> - 简短规则。
> - 要跟踪的证据或记录。
> - 风险 / 常见误区。
> - 下一步动作。

Example for PRV 184/184 node:

> 例如 PRV 184/184 节点：

- Type: decision / PRV commitment.
- Rule: from the PRV application date, look back 24 months; each 12-month block needs 184+ days in New Zealand as a resident.
- Evidence: travel records and resident status dates.
- Risk: counting by calendar year instead of actual application date.
- Next action: if a block is short, select a later application date or review other commitment paths.

> - 类型：decision / PRV commitment。
> - 规则：从 PRV 申请日往前倒推 24 个月，每个 12 个月区间需要 184+ 天在新西兰以 resident 身份居住。
> - 证据：出入境记录和 resident 身份日期。
> - 风险：按自然年算，而不是按实际申请日倒推。
> - 下一步：如果某个区间不够，选择更晚申请日，或研究其他 commitment 路径。

- Keep text short inside nodes.
- Put explanations into the right-hand detail panel.
- Use timeline and table sections only after the flowchart.
- Keep the official-source section compact.

> - 节点内文字要短。
