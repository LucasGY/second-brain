# E-commerce has never been bigger in the US: E-commerce now reflects a record 16.6% of total US retail sales. Put simply, nearly $1 out of every $6 spe...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-11 00:12

E-commerce has never been bigger in the US:

E-commerce now reflects a record 16.6% of total US retail sales.

Put simply, nearly $1 out of every $6 spent in US retail is now online.

By comparison, it was just 0.8% in Q1 2000, meaning activity has surged by over 20x since 2000.

In Q4 2025, online sales surged to a record $365.2 billion.

As a result, total US e-commerce sales for the full year 2025 were estimated at a record $1.23 trillion, up +5.4% from 2024.

Online shopping is replacing brick-and-mortar.
