**@投资TALK君** · 2026-04-11 21:47

RT BRICS News
Re High-level direct negotiations between the United States and Iran grew tense on Saturday in Pakistan's capital. 

The highest-level face-to-face meetings between the two countries since the 1979 Islamic Revolution, are mediated by Pakistani officials and aim to strengthen a fragile ceasefire that ended a six-week regional conflict. 

US Vice President JD Vance leads the American side, joined by special envoy Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner. Iran's delegation includes senior officials such as Parliamentary Speaker Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf. 

Sessions continued into late night with multiple rounds, including trilateral discussions. Reports cite rising tensions over control of the Strait of Hormuz, frozen Iranian assets, sanctions relief, and the ceasefire's scope regarding Lebanon. 

Heavy security measures remain in place across Islamabad. No agreement has been announced as of Saturday evening.
