# Global dealmaking is exceptionally strong: Global M&A transaction values jumped +18% YoY in Q1 2026, to $1.25 trillion, the largest Q1 total on record...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-10 01:48

Global dealmaking is exceptionally strong:

Global M&A transaction values jumped +18% YoY in Q1 2026, to $1.25 trillion, the largest Q1 total on record.

This includes Unilever's, $UL, $44.8 billion sale of its food business to McCormick, $MKC, and Sysco's, $SYY, $29.1 billion takeover of Jetro Restaurant Depot.

Global dealmaking has surged +$670 billion, or +116%, over the last 3 years.

Last quarter also saw 18 deals valued at $10+ billion, among the highest number announced in a single quarter in history.

As a result, global M&A transactions are on track to surpass a record ~$4.5 trillion full-year total seen in 2025.

Corporate dealmaking is incredibly hot.
