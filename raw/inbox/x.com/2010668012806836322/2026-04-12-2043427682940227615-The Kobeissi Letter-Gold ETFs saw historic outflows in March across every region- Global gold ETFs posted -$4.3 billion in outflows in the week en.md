# Gold ETFs saw historic outflows in March across every region: Global gold ETFs posted -$4.3 billion in outflows in the week ending March 27th, the 2nd...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-12 20:34

Gold ETFs saw historic outflows in March across every region:

Global gold ETFs posted -$4.3 billion in outflows in the week ending March 27th, the 2nd-largest on record, only below the -$4.6 billion seen the prior week.

North America led with -$1.6 billion, followed by Asia at -$1.5 billion, and Europe at -$1.2 billion.

This marks the 4th consecutive weekly withdrawal, the longest streak since Q1 2024.

Over this period, global gold ETFs have amassed -$12.2 billion in outflows, on track for the largest monthly withdrawal in history.

By comparison, the April 2013 record was -$8.7 billion.

The Iran War is redefining gold flows.
