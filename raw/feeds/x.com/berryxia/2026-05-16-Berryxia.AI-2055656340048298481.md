It is the birthplace of Fuxi culture and the ancient I Ching Bagua philosophy, home to age-old myths such as the legend of Nüwa mending the sky. 

Boasting profound historical and cultural heritage, it is the perfect destination for anyone fascinated by Chinese history who wishes to dive deep into ancient civilizations.

The city houses Maijishan Grottoes, one of China’s best-preserved grotto complexes. 

Though some famous statues have sustained damage over time, the site remains well worth visiting for its immense historical and cultural value. 

Tianshui’s cultural roots stretch far beyond the commonly known 5,000 years of Chinese civilization, dating all the way back 8,000 years.

This is an ideal time of year to travel here. You can fully immerse yourself in the rich local culture and taste a great variety of authentic regional delicacies.
