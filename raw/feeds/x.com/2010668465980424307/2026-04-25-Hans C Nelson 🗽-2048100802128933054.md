# Here's something for the @OpenClaw haters and skeptics to chew on. You may not like it. You may think it's stupid. You may severely disagree with some...

**@Hans C Nelson 🗽** · 2026-04-25 18:04

Here's something for the @OpenClaw haters and skeptics to chew on.

You may not like it. You may think it's stupid. You may severely disagree with some of the architectural decisions.

None of that matters.

OpenClaw is the JavaScript of open-source agent frameworks.

Yes, it started out kludgy and hacky.

But adoption mindshare and product velocity cover over a multitude of sins.

Now that this cat is out of the bag, there simply is no stopping it.

I say this as someone who has been running both Hermes and OpenClaw side by side.

I like both, and I have a lot of respect for what each team has done.

And I think there is plenty of room for both projects to succeed wildly.

But the cold hard fact of the matter is that OpenClaw has already achieved network effect & pace of innovation escape velocity and will continue to be the center of gravity for this ecosystem for the foreseeable future.
