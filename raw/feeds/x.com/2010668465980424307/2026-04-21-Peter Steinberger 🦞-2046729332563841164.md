OpenClaw 2026.4.20 🦞 A few other things in this release that didn’t make the main tweet:

🧠 GPT-5 / Codex is better at picking up SOUL/IDENTITY guidance now, so it should feel more like your weirdly capable little friend and less like a polished customer support automaton.
🤫 NO_REPLY is smarter now, so DMs are less likely to feel like your bot faceplanted mid-conversation while groups can still stay tastefully quiet.
🔄 /new and /reset now clear stale auto-pinned model/provider/auth state, so sessions are less likely to keep clinging to some random fallback choice from three mistakes ago.
🧭 Onboarding/setup got cleaner, so the wizard feels less like a suspicious terminal ritual and more like something a normal person can actually follow.
🧹 Session maintenance is stricter by default, which should help long-running gateways avoid slowly turning into a cursed attic full of old cron/session dust.
