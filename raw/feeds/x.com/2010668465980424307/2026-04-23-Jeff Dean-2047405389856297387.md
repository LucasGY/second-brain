# Re First, let's talk about TPU 8t, which is designed for large-scale training and inference throughput. The pod size is increased slightly to 9600 chi...

**@Jeff Dean** · 2026-04-23 20:00

Re First, let's talk about TPU 8t, which is designed for large-scale training and inference throughput.

The pod size is increased slightly to 9600 chips, and provides ~3X the FP4 performance per pod vs. Ironwood (8t has 121 exaflops/pod vs. 42.5 exaflops/pod for Ironwood).  In addition, the ICI network bandwidth is 2X higher per chip and the scale out datacenter networking is 4X higher per chip.

Importantly, this system also offers 2X the performance / watt, continuing the trend of significant energy effiency improvements that we've had for many generations of TPUs (8t offers ~60X the performance/watt as TPU v2).
