**@SemiAnalysis** · 2026-05-14 17:00

Re This Broadfly ICI network consists of 36 TPU Groups, each connected to an OCS layer with 32 1.6T DR4 QSFP per TPU group. Each TPU group contains 8 interconnected building blocks (BBs), with each BB having 8 1.6T AECs (2 per TPU) connecting to the 7 other BBs in the group, and one link doubled to the neighboring BB.

Within the BB, there are 2 interconnected PCBs of 2 TPUs each. The TPUs on the same PCB are directly connected with 1.6T bi-directional bandwidth. Then, both PCBs are connected using a 1.6T flyover DAC and a 1.6T intra-tray DAC.  (2/3)
