# The market has never been this dependent on tech: A record 115 companies in the S&P 500 now trade above a $100 billion market cap. This figure has nea...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-05-16 00:52

The market has never been this dependent on tech:

A record 115 companies in the S&P 500 now trade above a $100 billion market cap.

This figure has nearly TRIPLED since the 2020 pandemic low of 42.

By comparison, 20 companies were worth over $100 billion before the 2008 Financial Crisis, and just 8 at the 2008 low.

The technology sector leads with 31 firms above the $100 billion threshold.

This is followed by financials, industrials, and healthcare at 19, 16, and 16, respectively.

To put this into perspective, just 8 technology companies were valued at over $100 billion at the 2020 market low.

The US equity market has reached an unprecedented scale.
