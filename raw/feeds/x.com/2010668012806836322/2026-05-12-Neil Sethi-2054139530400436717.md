**@Neil Sethi** · 2026-05-12 10:00

Nightly email from @dailychartbook had an update on Goldman's CTA estimates so I thought I'd add their SPX estimates as I normally do which have them deteriorating slightly from last week's update which saw little change in all scenarios but a down month (where the selling is large):   

-a flat market is now expected to see +$0.1bn in buying over the next week and +$0.9bn over the next month;   
- an up market (>2 s/d's so a pretty big move higher) sees -$0.4bn of selling (because of the increased volatility) over the next week and +$0.2bn of buying over the next month  
 - a down market (more than -2.5 s/d's (so a big move lower)) sees +$0.3bn in buying over the next week (again oddly the most constructive price path over the next week) but -$31.9bn of selling over the next month (although that is down from -$36.8bn a week ago).
