**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-05-07 16:53

BREAKING: Investors bought +$8.7 billion of US equities last week, the first net purchase in 3 weeks.

ETFs accounted for +$6.8 billion of this total, the highest since data began in 2017, with single stocks contributing an additional +$1.9 billion.

8 of 11 sectors saw inflows, with Technology, Consumer Discretionary, and Materials posting outflows.

Institutional investors led the buying activity at +$6.9 billion, their first weekly purchase in 4 weeks.

At the same time, hedge funds bought +$1.2 billion while retail investors added +$602 million.

Institutional investors are piling into equities.
