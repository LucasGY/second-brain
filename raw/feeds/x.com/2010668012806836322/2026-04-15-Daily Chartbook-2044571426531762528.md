# RT Neil Sethi: I noted in the Week Ahead (https://neilsethi.substack.com/p/the-week-ahead-41226) that we had seen SPX earnings revisions turn the most...

**@Daily Chartbook** · 2026-04-15 10:15

RT Neil Sethi
I noted in the Week Ahead (https://neilsethi.substack.com/p/the-week-ahead-41226) that we had seen SPX earnings revisions turn the most negative in the week through April 3rd since last April, breaking a 10-week streak of positive revisions and seeing the 20-week moving average while firmly positive softening to the joint least since August (even as next 12 month EPS estimates continue to rise to new highs as they’ve done each week since the turn of the year).  That's in the first chart.

But apparently it's been even worse for the next 1000 stocks as Bespoke says the SPX 1500 has seen earnings revisions at -8.4%, the most negative in a year.  The good news as they note (via the @dailychartbook nightly email) is that reduced expectations are fuel for beats and "equities tend to perform better during earnings season versus when the revisions spread is positive."
