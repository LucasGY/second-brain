# Retail investors are leading the historic rally: Retail's favorite stocks are outperforming mutual fund favorite names by +11 percentage points so far...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-20 00:10

Retail investors are leading the historic rally:

Retail's favorite stocks are outperforming mutual fund favorite names by +11 percentage points so far in April.

This puts retail on track for their best relative month since November 2020 after 5 consecutive months of relative underperformance.

As a result, the basket of stocks most favored by retail investors has rallied +22% since late March.

Bullish retail investor sentiment is surging.
