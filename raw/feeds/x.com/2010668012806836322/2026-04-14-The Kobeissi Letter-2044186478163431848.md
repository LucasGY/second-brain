# The AI boom is fueling a historic surge in startup investment: Global startup investment surged +153% in Q1 2026, to a record $300 billion. This is al...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-14 22:50

The AI boom is fueling a historic surge in startup investment:

Global startup investment surged +153% in Q1 2026, to a record $300 billion.

This is already ~70% of all venture capital spending seen in the entire year of 2025.

This was driven by late-stage deals that spiked +281% QoQ, to a record $243 billion.

AI represented $242 billion, or ~80% of total global venture funding last quarter, up from 55% in Q1 2025.

By comparison, AI firms raised $215 billion in the full year of 2025.

This comes as just 4 US companies secured $188 billion, or 65% of the global total. 

OpenAI led at $122 billion, followed by Anthropic at $30 billion, xAI at $20 billion, and Waymo at $16 billion.

The AI race is accelerating.
