# Samsung Union: One Rally Already Hit Output. A Strike Is Next. Memory fabs down 18.4%, foundry lines down 58.1%, and this wasn't even a strike, just a...

**@SemiAnalysis** · 2026-04-30 02:00

Samsung Union: One Rally Already Hit Output. A Strike Is Next.

Memory fabs down 18.4%, foundry lines down 58.1%, and this wasn't even a strike, just a rally. The union is now threatening 18 consecutive days starting May 21 unless Samsung agrees to hand over roughly KRW 45tn in bonuses.

Foundry took the bigger hit because the lines are less automated. When workers walk, output collapses, and anything mid-process gets scrapped.

Realistically, this probably gets resolved before it fully escalates. Korean labor disputes tend to settle, and Samsung has too much riding on 2H supply commitments to let this drag. But the fact that a single rally moved the needle this much tells you something about how much leverage the union actually has sitting at the table.
