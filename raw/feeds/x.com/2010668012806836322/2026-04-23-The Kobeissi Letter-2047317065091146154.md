# US semiconductor stocks are extending their historic rally: The Semiconductor Index, $SOX, has gained for 16 consecutive trading sessions, the longest...

**@The Kobeissi Letter** · 2026-04-23 14:10

US semiconductor stocks are extending their historic rally:

The Semiconductor Index, $SOX, has gained for 16 consecutive trading sessions, the longest streak in history.

This has officially surpassed the previous record stretch of 15 days posted in 2014.

Over this period, $SOX has surged +38.7%, on track for its largest monthly gain since February 2000.

Semiconductor ETFs $SOXX and $SMH have attracted +$2.1 billion and +$3.4 billion in inflows so far in April.

Combined, they have pulled in +$5.5 billion, already surpassing any other full month of inflows on record.

The tech trade is back.
