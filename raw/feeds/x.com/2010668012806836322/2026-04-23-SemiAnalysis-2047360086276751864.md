# The more things change the more they stay the same. 30 years ago Jensen Huang tried desperately to convince TSMC to supply wafers for the emerging cat...

**@SemiAnalysis** · 2026-04-23 17:00

The more things change the more they stay the same. 30 years ago Jensen Huang tried desperately to convince TSMC to supply wafers for the emerging category of chips dedicated to rendering 3D graphics. Today, Jensen Huang is still fighting for more wafer supply from TSMC to produce more GPUs but for the purposes of AI model training and inference. Not only Nvidia, but the likes of Broadcom, AMD, MediaTek, and Amazon are in the same position. One thing in Nvidia's favor is Jensen's long relationship with TSMC. This comic was commissioned by Nvidia as a gift to Morris Chang on his retirement as TSMC CEO, commemorating the special relationship between the 2 companies.
